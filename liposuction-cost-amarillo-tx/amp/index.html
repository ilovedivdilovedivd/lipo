The result is already known before the operation on 3D-images obtained with the help of a special device.

When is liposuction necessary?

Young people, in order to get rid of extra pounds, first of all it is worth keeping to a diet and doing sports. But after a certain age threshold to lose weight, tighten up and get an aesthetic relief of the hips and abdomen, only physical exercises are sometimes simply impossible.

The best solution is liposuction. Problem areas for most people are the same: hips, stomach and chin. Unfortunately, the lower leg and forearm can not be corrected by liposuction in Amarillo.

TYPES AND METHODS OF LIPOSUCTION

The procedure is performed under local or general anesthesia. A plastic surgeon inserts a cannula (a thin tube) through small punctures and connects it to a vacuum device for pumping. Then the fat tissue is destroyed by the chosen method of liposuction.

Types of liposuction in Amarillo:

  1. Dry liposuction is the mechanical destruction of fat using a cannula. This method because of its traumatism came out of the use of plastic surgeons.
  2. Wet liposuction is performed using saline and adrenaline to facilitate pumping out fat cells.
  3. Water-jet liposuction is the separation of fat by a jet of water under high pressure.
  4. Laser liposuction damages the fat cells by heat treatment and tightens the skin.

FEATURES OF LIPOSUCTION

Around the liposuction wanders a lot of myths among people far from plastic surgery. It is believed that the operation is dangerous, too difficult to rehabilitate and generally ineffective. But this is nothing more than a horror story. Plastic surgery is developed by leaps and bounds, and now the course of liposuction is controlled by high-precision equipment. Before the operation, the patient has the necessary tests and consults with specialists, which also reduces the risk of complications. Of course, any surgical intervention requires recovery.

The recovery period, lasting about a month, involves wearing compression underwear, proper nutrition and lack of exercise. Thanks to modern technology during liposuction and following the recommendations of the doctor, pain, swelling and bruising disappear quickly and leave behind a slender silhouette.

Of course, one should not rely on equipment and own organism alone. After all, the main thing in plastic surgery is the surgeon’s hands. It is the professionalism of the doctor that determines the course of the operation, the result, and further recovery.

And here it is very important to choose a clinic that specializes in body sculpting, because a surgeon working with a person will not always perform liposuction finely.
