Do silicone implants need to be moved every 2 years and changed every 10?
Where are you getting your information?
Sep 2, 2021
The difference in feel between saline and silicone augmented breasts depends on how thick your tissue is and how big the implant is. The thinner your tissue the more likely you will feel/see ripples with saline implants. Gel implants don’t need to be “moved” every two years and with an 8% leak rate at ten years (Allergan implants) I advise my patients to not change them every ten years. My patients routinely get 15-20 years out of their implants. Hope this helps, good luck!
