Liposuction In Omaha Nebraska

If you can not lose weight, no diet helps to make a flat stomach, remove excess fat from the hips, then you will help liposuction.

Liposuction in Omaha allows you to eliminate excess fat deposits and practically will not leave postoperative traces. Depending on the area, surgical liposuction can take place under both local and general anesthesia and have a duration of 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the amount of intervention. These factors directly affect the cost of liposuction.

After liposuction, the patient should undergo a recovery treatment, staying in the hospital for 1-2 days, which also affects the price of liposuction of the abdomen and other areas.

The recovery period is 3-4 weeks and in some cases requires special compression underwear. Completely evaluate the results of the operation is possible only after 1-6 months.

Many clinics of plastic surgery offer today different methods of liposuction without surgery, in particular laser and ultrasound liposuction. These are less traumatic, safe methods that do not require a long recovery period.
