Kurt Kuhlman

Name: Kurt Kuhlman
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 35
Biography: Dr. Kurt Kuhlman graduated from The Ohio State University in 1986. From 1986 to 1988, he worked as a Physical Therapist and was also an anatomy and physiology teaching assistant and tutor at Ohio State. He attended Ohio University College of Osteopathic Medicine and graduated first in his class in 1992. He completed a 4-year Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation residency program at The Ohio State University, where he was the chief resident in 1995-1996. Dr. Kuhlman is a Botox expert and is employed by Allergan to train physicians on proper technique. He has been injecting botulinum toxins (Botox) since 1996 for a variety of medical conditions including spasticity, dystonia, chronic migraine, hyperhydrosis, blepharospasm, neuromuscular disorders, Raynaud's disease, etc. He has extensive training in cosmetic injectables including botulinum toxins (Botox, Dysport, Xeomin), dermal fillers, threads and Kybella, and using multiple lasers for all your cosmetic needs.
