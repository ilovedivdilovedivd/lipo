Virginia Liposuction Prices & Before After Photos

Liposuction in Virginia is one of the most frequently used operations for body correction. It is most often used as a method of eliminating local fatty adipose tissue (so-called “fat traps”).

Thinking out loud

There are different versions of liposuction depending on the method of action on adipose tissue (tumescent, laser, ultrasound), but in principle liposuction consists in the vacuum removal through the thin tubes (cannulas) of the destroyed adipose tissue.

The main zones of local fatty deposits (traps) differ slightly in men and women, but are mainly located on the abdomen, waist, back, areas of the inner and outer surfaces of the thighs, buttocks, upper parts of the hands, chin, neck.

Liposuction in Virginia should not be considered as a way to combat common obesity and cellulite. Liposuction is most suitable for patients whose weight is stable and close to normal, and with excess weight – it is recommended first to reduce and stabilize the weight.

The best results after liposuction are observed at the age of about 40 years, which is related to the tone and elasticity of the skin and its ability to contract after the operation.

Often, liposuction in Virginia is used as a supplement to one or another type of operation. For example, liposuction of the chin – as a stage of facial plastic surgery, liposuction of the back, abdomen, waist – in combination with abdominoplasty.

How it’s done:

During the consultation, you will discuss with the doctor the problems and possible solutions.

The operation can be performed under local anesthesia, but for easier patient comfort, especially for large areas of exposure, use a light intravenous anesthesia.

In the zones designated before the operation, the surgeon introduces a special solution containing a local anesthetic, an adrenaline solution in appropriate proportions and volumes. After a short time, special thin tubes (cannulas) cause destruction and vacuum aspiration of adipose tissue.

Small incisions of 2-3 mm in length necessary to perform liposuction can not be closed.

After the operation, the patient wears special compression underwear.

Depending on the volume of liposuction and the type of possible other performed operations, the patient can leave the clinic in a few hours or the next day.

How to prepare for liposuction?

  • General blood test;
  • Coagulogram (coagulation system): fibrinogen, prothrombin;
  • Biochemical blood test (total protein, glucose, bilirubin, urea, creatinine);
  • Hepatitis B, C, HIV, syphilis;
  • Blood type, Rh factor;
  • ECG, chest X-ray (indicated)

Postoperative period and recovery

  • Compliance with your recommendations for managing the postoperative period is an integral part of a good and long-term result.
  • Try to organize in advance the most comfortable stay at home after the operation.
  • Pain sensations are minimal, as a rule, do not require taking painkillers.
  • Swelling, minimal bruising may persist for up to 2 weeks after surgery. The closest result will be visible after 1.5 months, and the final result will be seen 3-6 months after the operation.
  • Exclude physical activity for 2 weeks after surgery, wearing compression underwear for 2-4 weeks.
  • 1-2 times a day it is required to process postoperative sutures with solutions of antiseptics (miramistin, chlorhexidine).
  • Any changes in the state of health, in the postoperative area, should be discussed immediately with the doctor by phone, in order to take appropriate measures, if necessary.
