Doctors

Greg Ratliff, MD

Name: Greg Ratliff, MD
Statement: Dr. Greg Ratliff, a distinguished board-certified plastic surgeon, serves as the Medical Director of the Plastic Surgery Center of Tulsa and Inject, an Aesthetics Bar. With an impressive track record spanning more than three decades, Dr. Ratliff is renowned for delivering personalized care and achieving remarkable results for his patients. His areas of expertise lie in breast enhancement and body procedures, including sought-after mommy makeover plastic surgery and precise body contouring through liposuction.

Dr. Ratliff maintains a steadfast commitment to staying on the cutting edge of breast implant research, ensuring his patients benefit from the latest advancements in treatment options. He earned his MD degree from the University of Oklahoma and completed rigorous residencies in plastic and reconstructive surgery at the esteemed Medical University of South Carolina. As a testament to his exceptional qualifications, he holds board certification from the American Board of Plastic Surgery and remains an active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

Furthermore, Dr. Ratliff has contributed significantly to the field, serving on the Mentor LEAD Advisory Council for over a decade. He also imparts his expertise as a faculty member at the Osler Institute, where he assists fellow doctors in preparing for their board certification examinations.
