Philadelphia Liposuction Prices

Liposuction of the abdomen is by far the most effective way to get rid of belly fat. This procedure takes a special place in aesthetic surgery, because:

– Excess fat in the abdomen are observed quite often, both in women and men;
– The technique of removing fat from the abdomen is relatively simple;
– The procedure of liposuction in Philadelphia gives a noticeable and persistent result;
– Minimal scars even when removing fat from extensive areas.

The main indication for abdominal liposuction is the presence of local fat accumulations in the abdominal region, which disrupt the body contour and remain even with a significant decrease in weight.

The best aesthetic effect when removing fat in the abdomen can be achieved if there is a sufficiently pronounced fatty deposits.

This is due to the fact that excessive roundness of the abdomen is sometimes caused by a significant stretching of the muscles, and not at all by excess fat.

And in this case, abdominoplasty is indicated , which will help change the muscle contour and remove excess skin from the abdomen.

During the evaluation of the degree of surgical intervention, there are:

  • Liposuction of small volume (removal of fat to 0.66 gallon);
  • Liposuction of a large volume (removal of fat from 0.66 gallon to 1.32 gallon);
  • Liposuction of extra-large volume (removal of fat over 1.32 gallon).

The cost of liposuction of the abdomen in Philadelphia depends on the volume of the operation and is discussed during the consultation.

With a small volume of fat removed, liposuction of the abdomen can be performed under local anesthesia. In all other cases, the procedure for removing fat from the abdomen is performed under general anesthesia and requires the patient to stay in the hospital for a further period (for 1-3 days or more).

The volume of fat removed is always limited to reduce the risk of complications after liposuction of the abdomen.

The maximum possible amount of fat removed from the abdomen (during one procedure) is discussed in detail with a plastic surgeon in a full-time consultation.

As a rule, taking a decision on the amount of intervention, each surgeon takes as a basis personal experience.

After abdominoplasty for 3-4 weeks, you need to wear compression underwear, take medications prescribed by the surgeon and visit the clinic for examination and dressing.

In the first days after the liposuction of the abdomen, general weakness, fever and minor pain are possible.

In this period, the patient is recommended to rest more. Also during the recovery period, there may be swelling of the soft tissues, bruising and a feeling of numbness in the abdominal area.

These symptoms will gradually decrease and disappear within 4-6 weeks. The preliminary result after liposuction of the abdomen can be assessed no earlier than 1.5-2 months after the procedure. The final aesthetic effect is observed after 6-8 months.
