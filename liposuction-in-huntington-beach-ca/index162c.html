Liposuction In Huntington Beach CA

Liposuction in Huntington Beach, CA is the removal of fat deposits from the designated areas of the patient through the cannula – a special suction tube of small diameter – with a vacuum pump.

A little history and facts about liposuction.

The popularity of liposuction in the whole world is confirmed by specific figures. Currently, plastic surgeons perform more than 450,000 surgeries for pumping excess fat per year.

The first such operation was carried out 40 years ago – in 1974. Modern methods have been significantly improved over the past period of time, and today surgical manipulations in the process of liposuction are associated with lower risks to health and less pain.

Features of the procedure for liposuction

  1. When pumping fat by liposuction, even in large areas, there are practically no traces of the operation (or barely discernible).
  2. The operation is well tolerated and with continued follow-up of the surgeon’s recommendations gives a lasting and lasting result.
  3. Liposuction can be performed as a separate operation or in combination with other aesthetic operations – for example, together with mastopexy (breast reduction) or abdominoplasty. In addition, liposuction can also be performed immediately in several problem areas.

The time of the operation is from 1 to 3 hours – depending on the number of zones and the volume of fat removed. If you want to adjust several zones at once, the operation, accordingly, will take more time.

Contraindications

Liposuction is not performed when the patient (or patient) “has” the following health problems:

  • varicose veins, thrombophlebitis;
  • diseases of internal organs;
  • diabetes;
  • oncological diseases;
  • bleeding disorders;
  • infectious diseases;
  • cardiovascular diseases.

What is Liposuction?

Liposuction in Huntington Beach, CA is an operation to remove excess adipose tissue that accumulates in the problem areas of a person in order to adjust its shape. Most often, adipose tissue is present on the abdomen, thighs, buttocks, calves, in the region of the knee joints and in the chin area.

But liposuction makes the body only slightly better, and it will not be perfectly slim. With the help of such a plastic surgery can not cope with obesity, cellulite and stretch marks. To your body has been adjusted as efficiently as possible, it is necessary not only to carry out such an aesthetic surgery, but also to change the quality of nutrition.

When you get rid of excess fat deposits in all problem areas using traditional methods such as massage, exercise or diet, it is no longer possible, the next step is liposuction. In those places where the operation was performed, more skin remains. If necessary, excess skin is removed.

Nowadays, there are three types of liposuction:

  1. power assisted liposuction,
  2. ultrasonic,
  3. laser.

The choice of technique depends on where the effect is required, what is the structure of the fat folds, what are the features of the human body. Anesthesia can also be different.

If a patient needs to remove a small amount of adipose tissue, local anesthesia is used. If you have to treat more than three parts of the body, anesthesia is used. The time of the operation is about three to four hours.

It is possible to remove from 1 lb to 26 lb of fat at a time. The first three days after such surgery (except for laser liposuction) the patient should be in a hospital under the supervision of doctors. Seams are removed after seven to ten days.

The entire first month is prohibited from performing any physical activities. Sports, tanning and sauna are also contraindicated. You must always wear compression underwear or bandage all the time.
