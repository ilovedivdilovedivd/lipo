Newport Beach Liposuction Surgery – Prices, Before And After Photos

What is Liposuction? This is an operational correction of the shape and volume of the body due to the removal of fat deposits.

With the help of liposuction in Newport Beach, you can remove fat deposits on the abdomen, waist, buttocks, hips, legs, knees, shoulder area, scapular and peri-ribbed zone, chin.

It is usually used in cases where it is impossible to remove excess fat deposits with the help of diets and sports: for example, liposuction of the abdomen helps to get rid of deposits in the abdominal zone, which is difficult to work out with the help of physical exercises.

In this case, the operation for liposuction of the abdomen gives an immediate effect, in contrast to sports activities, massage and other physical methods.

Laser liposuction in Newport Beach is suitable for people who are overweight or who have local fat deposits. We can carry out both correction of a certain part of the body, and correct the contours of the body as a whole.

The procedure of liposuction is to insert under the skin a special surgical tube – the cannula, through which the surgeon punctures the laser beam on the fat deposits. In this case, the envelope of fat cells is destroyed, and subsequently they are removed from the body in a natural way.

This process can be accelerated – for example, with the help of the vacuum massage procedure. Often, this operation is combined with the procedure of lipofilling – transplanting your own fat tissue into those areas where the face or body needs volume.

As a rule, this applies to liposuction of the abdomen – this is the zone most often becoming a donor. The allocated fat can be used as a material for modeling other parts of the body: chest, buttocks, lips, zygomatic and temporal zone.

Stages of the operation

  • The operation is performed under general anesthesia.
  • The duration of the operation is from 30 minutes to 3 hours, depending on the number of zones.
  • Through micro punctures by the introduction of a special cannula, suction of excess adipose tissue is performed.
  • The amount of fat allocated in one operation should not exceed 5% of the total body weight.
  • After the operation, it is mandatory to wear compression underwear for 3-4 weeks with the exception of any physical activity.
  • After surgery, there are no scars on the body.
  • Full recovery is 4 weeks.
  • Repeated operation can be performed after 3-4 months.

It should be understood that non-compliance with the norms of a healthy lifestyle, proper nutrition will provoke the rapid disappearance of the effect of liposuction. The prices for this operation are quite high, and it would be unreasonable to repeat it again and again, without struggling with the main cause of excess weight. The operation does not guarantee the disposal of excess fatty deposits forever – laser liposuction allows you to quickly simulate a new form of your body, which you will need to support yourself.
