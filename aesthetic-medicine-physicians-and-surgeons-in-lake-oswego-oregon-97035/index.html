Statement: Dr. Jerry Darm, MD has over 20 years of experience providing the best in aesthetic medicine to patients in the Lake Oswego and greater Portland, Oregon area. He is recognized for his expertise and skill performing a variety of procedures, especially liposuction and laser skin tightening.

Dr. Darm received his MD at Oregon Health and Science University, where he studied emergency medicine (a specialty in which he is board certified).
