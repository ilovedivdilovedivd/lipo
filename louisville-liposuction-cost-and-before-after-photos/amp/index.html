Louisville Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos

To change the nature of fat deposits in one or more problem areas of the body, such as the hips, abdomen, buttocks, such a cosmetic operation as liposuction in Louisville is widely practiced.

The visual effect of such a procedure can be more noticeable in the body than with the loss of the same fat by any other method, despite the fact that liposuction is not a method of treating obesity.

A sedentary, sedentary lifestyle, malnutrition, and genetic predisposition often leads to overweight.

With the help of the procedure of ultrasonic liposuction, one can correct individual problem areas. However, this procedure is not a complete alternative to all these problems.

“Fat traps” are present in the body of each person. It is in these places that adipose tissue tends to accumulate forever, despite any diet and exercise.

The purpose of the procedure is to create the ideal contour of your body, and not to reduce excess weight. To achieve this goal, both classical mechanical, radio frequency, and laser liposuction are used.

Indications for surgery:

Fat deposits, which can be got rid of after the procedure of liposuction, arise mainly in the following areas of the body:

  • Chin;
  • Neck;
  • Belly;
  • Lateral surfaces of the back;
  • External and internal surfaces of the thighs;
  • The internal surfaces of the knees.

Typically, most women who turn to this method, want to get rid of the so-called “riding breeches” (liposuction hips). No less popular direction is getting rid of fat deposits on the abdomen and waist (liposuction of the abdomen), buttocks, forearms, calves, back, knees and so on.

As for men, as a rule, they want to get rid of the deposits on the neck (as well as ultrasonic liposuction of the chin), waist, abdomen, chest, back and buttocks.

Contraindication to the operation of ultrasonic liposuction (including thighs and chin) is the presence of acute chronic diseases, serious diseases of internal organs, circulatory diseases and diabetes mellitus. Before the operation, patients for 10 days are prohibited from taking various drugs that affect blood clotting and smoking.

Also it is required to have necessary test and or take place procedure of clarification of an intestine. The date of the operation is appointed based on the results of all tests and after checking for contraindications.

Operation description:

The duration of the operation is 30-90 min. The final time is determined depending on the site on which the procedure is performed, as well as on the amount of adipose tissue that will be removed. With small amounts of fat removal, local anesthesia is used, and larger operations are performed under general anesthetic.

Before the beginning of the operation, the doctor marks the zone on which the surgical procedure will be performed, and the operation is performed on the newest specialized equipment with microscopic incisions from 2 to 5 mm.

The incision on the body in the previously marked region is less than 2.0-5.0 mm in length. Through it, fat is sucked out using a cannula with a diameter of 3 mm, which is attached to the vacuum pump.

Before removing a small amount of adipose tissue (submental liposuction in Louisville), the fat is destroyed by the mechanical movement of the cannula forward-back.

In this case, nerves and vessels are not damaged by using a cannula with a rounded end.

Laser and radiofrequency liposuction

Ultrasonic liposuction of the thighs and other problematic parts of the body follows the goal not only of removing excess fat deposits and reducing the total body weight, but at the same time a perfect figure is formed, so at the moment this operation is increasingly called lipo-modelling.

The introduction of the latest medical equipment and instruments using laser and radiofrequency effects, which are now widely used in many areas of plastic and aesthetic surgery, is associated with the emergence of high technologies. The RF and laser irradiation contribute to more rapid healing with minimal scarring and virtually imperceptible as the least traumatic cut tissue.

These devices due to their versatility and multifunctionality are used for a variety of plastic operations. The greatest use of laser technology found during the procedures lipomodelling (including hips), because as a result it is necessary to achieve not only getting rid of excess fat, achieve a smooth surface of the body.

Among the advantages of conducting operations using radiofrequency and laser liposuction techniques (chin, thighs, buttocks and other body parts), less pain and a shorter postoperative recovery period can be distinguished.
