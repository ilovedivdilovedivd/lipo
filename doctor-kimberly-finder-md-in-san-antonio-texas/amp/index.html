Laser Peeling after Botox?
Treatments after Botox
Sep 27, 2018
If you've had Botox, you can safely do other procedures the very next day. Some procedures can be done the same day, but only if the y're done first and the Botox last. If something creates swelling, it may not be wise to do Botox, or if massaging is part of the after care, it's very important not to manipulate the Botox-treated areas. It's important to see an experienced dermatologist who understands what procedures can be combined.
CO2 or Infini for acne scars, which one has a better result?
Infini RF or CO2 for acne scars?
Sep 27, 2018
I've used fractionated CO2 laser for many years for acne scars and I've also used Infini RF in recent years. I'm very impressed with Infini RF. In my experience Infini RF is far superior. But Infini is more for the mid to deep dermis and I also use LaseMD thulium laser simultaneously for more superficial coverage and find that this combination is very effective.
