Liposuction Plano Price And Before & After Pictures

Liposuction in Plano TX is a modern high-tech procedure, or rather a whole range of multidirectional procedures for eliminating excess body fat throughout the body. To date, plastic surgeons offer liposuction for the abdomen, hips, sides, shins, forearms and even back.

The main task of liposuction is to get rid of excess fat deposits located locally. Doctors call them “fat traps”, these are the places on the body that are hard to correct with diets and sports, and therefore worry even the most slender and athletic. Plastic surgery is able to remedy the situation.

Liposuction is suitable for both women and men. It should be noted that liposuction is by no means a way to lose weight, do not try to use it to lose weight or solve the problem of obesity.

Types and possibilities of liposuction

Practically every modern clinic of plastic surgery offers several types of liposuction: vacuum, laser, water jet.

Vacuum liposuction in Plano

Vacuum fat liposuction involves pumping it under vacuum pressure. To this method of liposuction, doctors resort less and less, in connection with the appearance of more effective and sparing methods.

Laser liposuction

Liposuction by laser is one of the most modern techniques. It involves the destruction of fat cells of fat with a laser beam. The technique is ideal for correcting body parts with a thin and delicate skin, in particular, the face and neck, ankles and lower legs.

Water-jet assisted liposuction

Water jet liposuction is a less traumatic procedure than vacuum. In the operated area, under pressure, a solution is applied, exfoliating the fat cells, then the crushed fat is removed through the surgical cannula. Water-jet liposuction allows you to remove up to 1.84 gallon of fat at a time.

Preparing for liposuction

Before the operation, it is desirable to reduce weight, if there is an excess. For this purpose, you can even sit for a while on a diet, give up smoking and taking alcoholic drinks for three to four weeks before the procedure.

One week before liposuction, it is necessary to stop taking anticoagulants (drugs that affect the composition and coagulability of the blood).

Anesthesia

The concept of liposuction combines quite different procedures in complexity. So the method of anesthesia directly depends on the specific operation: its area and the amount of intervention. On medium-sized sites, you can manage local anesthesia and intravenous anesthesia, with liposuction of the abdomen or thighs a deeper form of sedation is used.

Recovery period

Many are concerned about the excess skin after liposuction. With a good skin turgor and young age, the skin will be tightened independently, without additional procedures. If the tone of the skin is lowered, the patient’s age is solid, the doctor will prescribe special procedures to reduce skin, for some time it will be necessary to wear compression underwear. The very first results of liposuction will be noticeable immediately, on the day of surgery, the final aesthetic effect can be assessed not earlier than in a couple of months. Approximately two weeks after the procedure, you can go back to sports exercises, jogging, dancing.

Frequently asked Questions

Having decided on liposuction, the future patient will inevitably ask two practical questions: where to make liposuction and how much does it cost? The cost of liposuction varies depending on the zone of liposuction, the volume of fat deposits, the technique of the operation. The clinic, where liposuction is done, is very much today.

Prices for liposuction consist of how many zones will be affected by the procedure: liposuction of the entire body is several times more expensive than liposuction of the shins or thighs. Prices for laser liposuction also differ from the prices for waterjet or dry liposuction. Laser liposuction is done with the help of modern and expensive equipment.
