Liposuction In Thousand Oaks CA

Liposuction of the abdomen is a plastic surgery that allows you to get rid of fat deposits in the abdomen. It is on the abdomen most often occurs the deposition of adipose tissue.

Liposuction in Thousand Oaks is performed to eliminate local accumulation of fat from the folds on the abdomen, the inner surface of the hips, back and shoulders.

Non-radical surgical interventions are called contour plasty. The reason for applying to a plastic surgeon can be the presence of a “widow” hump (fat deposits in the upper back).

The stability of the obtained results is ensured by the fact that the number of fat cells in a person with the course of life remains unchanged, so their removal does not entail restoration.

Indications

Excess of fatty deposits in the abdomen, which break the contour of the body. These deposits can remain, even with a large volume of lost mass, affecting the appearance and self-esteem.

Contraindications

  • Pregnancy and lactation
  • Decompensated diabetes mellitus
  • Serious cardiovascular diseases
  • Serious cardiac or mental problems
  • Malignant neoplasms and recovery period after chemotherapy courses
  • Acute and chronic infectious diseases
  • Violation of blood clotting
  • Exacerbation of systemic diseases of connective tissue, such as lupus erythematosus, rheumatism, glomerulonephritis and so on.
  • Complicated form of varicose veins: thrombosis, thrombophlebitis and so on.

Methodology

There are different methods of destruction of adipose tissue: mechanical, ultrasound, laser, water jet. Each of them has its advantages and disadvantages and is selected for each patient individually, proceeding from the specifics of a particular case.

Fat is removed using a special tool – a thin cannula, which almost does not leave a trace. At the end of the operation, the puncture marks are sealed with a special adhesive tape.

Depending on the volume, you can see the effect of liposuction either immediately or (at very small volumes) 3-4 weeks after the procedure (after swelling subsides). The duration of liposuction is from 1 to 3 hours.

Recovery

  • Stay in the hospital is outpatient or 1 day.
  • Mandatory wearing of compression underwear for a month;
  • Seams removal after 7 days;
  • Do not recommend physical activity for 2-3 weeks;
  • It is not recommended to visit the sauna, solarium before the removal of stitches.
