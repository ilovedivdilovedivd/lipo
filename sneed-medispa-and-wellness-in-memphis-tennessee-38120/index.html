I love my Restylane Lyft, but now under my eyes is hollow with wrinkles, what can I do?
tear troughs
Oct 4, 2018
Hi and thank you for your question. Restylane Lyft was a great filler choice as evidence by your amazing cheeks! I would suggest adding a filler to your under eye area (tear troughs) which will improve the hollowness.
Sometimes dynamic wrinkles (wrinkles present when making a face) to the under eye can be treated with a neurotoxin (Botox, Xeomin, Dysport). This is a tricky area, so make sure you are being treated by a reputable injector. Best of luck.
If the treatment of kybella post 6 weeks didn't work at all should I consider neck tight?
6 weeks post kybella
Oct 4, 2018
Hi and thank you for your question. If firmness is still present 6 weeks after Kybella, you are most likely still experiencing jpost injection swelling. Swelling can be present for up to 12 weeks after Kybella treatment. Firmness and nodules are also common after Kybella, which can be gently massaged. Kybella usually take 3-6 treatments before results are noted. Don't just your results too harshly until the swelling/firmness subsides.

how long does Xeomin really last

Hi and thank you for the question. Like Botox, Xeomin typically lasts 3-5 months, however the higher your metabolism, the faster you metabolize out the product. In other words, if you are active with a high metabolism, the Xeomin will not last as long. It is rare to become resistant to Xeomin. Lump and uneven texture under eyes after restylane fillers. Is this normal? Will it go away?
Swelling after filler
Oct 3, 2018
Hello and thank you for your question. It is common to have swelling and bruising for 2 weeks or longer after dermal filler injections. This is especially true after tear trough injections. It's difficult at this point to tell whether the swelling is from soft tissue edema from the treatment or from the Restylane itself. I would give it 2 full weeks before judging it too harshly. Continue to apply cold compress minutes on, minutes off for several hours each day.
