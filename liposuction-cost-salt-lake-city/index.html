Abdominal liposuction

Turning to a plastic surgeon, do not confuse liposuction – a gentle minimally invasive procedure for fat removal, with abdominoplasty – an extensive operation in the lower abdomen, during which the fat deposits are eliminated, abdominal muscles are tightened and excess skin is excised (skin fold).

Through the hose connected to the cannula, the fat is pumped out into a special container.

With the classical liposuction of the abdomen in the natural folds of the skin, several small incisions are made through which a cannula is inserted into the body – a thin hollow steel tube with apertures and a tip connected to the aspiration (vacuum) apparatus.

The destruction of fat tissue occurs as a result of its reciprocating movements.

Liposuction of the abdomen is done every day, both for women and for men. Which of the types of this procedure is performed – classical by dry, wet or tumescent technique, laser, ultrasound or radio wave, is determined individually in each case by the doctor and patient.

Liposuction of hips

Liposuction of the hips allows to remove localized fat deposits on their front surface, inside and back side, in the upper part near the inguinal fold, below the buttocks in the area of the “saddle bag”. Depending on the number and volume of treated areas, the operation is performed under general anesthesia or local anesthesia.

Immediately after it is finished, compression clothes are worn on the patient. The result of liposuction is visible almost immediately, although bruises and swelling after surgery completely disappear only after 1-2 months.

Liposuction of the hips is recommended for patients with a good skin tone and suffering from excess fat in this area. Contraindications for the procedure are cardiovascular and autoimmune diseases, hypertension, diabetes mellitus, blood clotting disorders, skin infections, pregnancy and lactation.

Liposuction of the chin

Due to the fact that physical exercises and cosmetic procedures are extremely ineffective in the chin area, liposuction of the chin is the only way to get rid of fat accumulated there. Only in this way it is possible to completely remove the skin-fat fold under the lower jaw (the so-called “second chin”) and return the profile to the former sharpness.

Liposuction of the chin is performed in a small area of the body under local anesthesia and is one of the safest procedures for removing subcutaneous fat with a very low probability of complications. Skin incisions 1-2 mm are located in the folds of the “double chin” and, when healing, become completely invisible. After 5-7 days after surgery, the patient can return to normal life.

Liposuction of knees and legs

Liposuction of the legs involves adjusting the silhouette of the lower body from the hips to the feet. Fat deposits, depending on the need, are removed from the hips, knees, calves, shins. The duration of the recovery period is 3-5 weeks. Since the main vessels and nerves of the leg pass in the popliteal fossa, liposuction of the knees should be performed with special care and caution. We strongly recommend that you do not save on your health and go to a clinic with a good reputation.

The removed fat cells are not renewed, therefore the liposuction concerns to single procedures and the effect from it is saved during all life of the patient. If you are experiencing moral or physical discomfort due to the presence of excess fat deposits – consider the possibility to contact a plastic surgeon and get rid of them once and for all.

The procedure of liposuction is carried out quickly and with a guarantee of the result, and its cost is quite comparable with the price of the course of injecting and hardware effects, the effectiveness of which can not be predicted.

Preoperative examination

Preoperative examination for general anesthesia:

  • General blood analysis
  • Sugar of blood
  • Blood type, Rh factor
  • Clotting time
  • Prothrombin index
  • Wasserman reaction (RW)
  • HIV
  • Hepatitis B, C
  • General urine analysis
  • ECG
  • Chest X-ray

Recovery after liposuction:

After a successful liposuction is recommended to refrain from the following activities:

  • sunbathing in the solarium or on the beach
  • drinking alcohol
  • Smoke
  • limitation of physical activity
  • wearing compression underwear

Rapid recovery after liposuction is possible if you stick to the prescriptions of a plastic surgeon. In addition, you will be recommended:

  1. applying a cold compress
  2. Taking anti-inflammatory drugs.
