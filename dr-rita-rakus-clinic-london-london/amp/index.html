Rita Rakus, MBBS

Name: Rita Rakus, MBBS
Statement: Dr. Rita Rakus, MBBS is a London-based physician with over 25 years of aesthetic medical experience. She has developed her practice into the largest solo non-surgical practice in the United Kingdom, and specializes in non-surgical skin tightening, body contouring, advanced lasers, and other aesthetic treatments.

She received her medical degree from MBBS Sydney University in Australia. Afterward, she pursued and completed her advanced certification in all non-surgical aesthetic treatments.

Dr. Rakus has been the recipient of many awards and accolades, including the Solta Black Diamond Award, Pelleve Glide-Safe Award, Ulthera Award, and Ultracel Award. She is a founding member of the Fellow of The British College of Aesthetic Medicine, and is a global ambassador for HydraFacial, BTL, EMSculpt, Emsella, and Vanquish.

Dr. Rakus’ award-winning practice in London is considered one of the world’s leading medical clinics. It boasts an impressive roster of the anti-ageing industry’s latest technological advancements, including EmSculpt, CoolSculpting, Ultherapy, Thermage, filler, neuromodulators, nonsurgical facelifts and more.

This broad offering, exceptional skill, and a welcoming environment make Dr. Rakus’ aesthetic clinic in Knightsbridge, London a top destination for anyone seeking rejuvenation and wellness treatments.
