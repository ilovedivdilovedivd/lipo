Doctors

John A. Standefer Jr., MD, FACS

Name: John A. Standefer Jr., MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. John A. Standefer Jr. is a nationally recognized, double board-certified facial plastic surgeon with over 38 years in practice. Exceptionally skilled in facial plastics and noninvasive aesthetic treatments, Dr. Standefer specializes in facelifts, neck lifts, eyelid surgery, and rhinoplasty.

“In my 30-plus years of experience, I have completed more than 10,000 successful facial surgeries, some of which have been combined resurfacing laser treatments,” says Dr. Standefer. “Using local anesthesia or IV sedation, I achieve natural-looking results using the most advanced surgical techniques.”

At Fresh Image, his boutique practice and accredited surgery center in Dallas, Texas, he and his staff also offer a wide range of noninvasive facial rejuvenation treatments, including Botox, dermal fillers, Broadband Light (BBL) photofacials, FaceTite, and laser resurfacing.

After earning his undergraduate degree from Texas A&M University, Dr. Standefer attained his medical degree at the University of Texas Health Science Center. Then, he completed both an internship in general surgery and a residency in otolaryngology at the University of Texas Health Science Center at San Antonio.

Dr. Standefer is board certified by both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. He is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS) and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

In addition to his impressive medical credentials, Dr. Standefer has received numerous awards. He is a RealSelf 500 recipient, TOP DOC last several years, and in 2019 won 2nd place in the prestigious International Euromedicom Conference for Best Surgical Rejuvenation. He has also been a presenter for the AAFPRS and International Academy of Facial Plastics.
