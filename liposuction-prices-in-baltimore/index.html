FEATURES OF THE PROCEDURE

Multi-stage, when in one session you can remove only about 1.32-1.58 gallons of fat, provided that the weight of the patient is 132lb-154lb. If fat is to be removed in large quantities, the operation should be divided into several stages.

Corrective liposuction in Baltimore is the removal of excess fat deposits under the skin. However, liposuction can not be considered a method of extremely fast weight loss. Its main task is to correct the shape of the body.

Liposuction will not help with obesity, since it is impossible to remove excess deposits throughout the body. There is no possibility of eliminating the reason for completeness.

Destroying fat. This is the goal of any method of liposuction. The procedure is an effective reduction of the number of fat cells.

Safe – to protect from bleeding, damage to nerve endings and vessels use special solutions that are injected on the eve of lipoaspiration. They also have an analgesic effect.

Liposuction techniques

I. Vacuum liposuction

Essence: a tube is inserted under the skin (into the cellulose), from it the hose leads to the vacuum apparatus. In this method, fatty tissue is destroyed by vacuum and sucked through the hose. The advantage of the method is that it has a high efficiency, while that for such a procedure as vacuum liposuction, the prices are lower than for the operation for removing fat by ultrasound or a laser methods.

II. Ultrasonic liposuction

Essence: ultrasound destroys fat accumulation. The removal of fat is done by vacuum. Plus the method is smaller in diameter punctures in the skin.

Minus of the method: ultrasound has an effect on neighboring organs.

III. Laser liposuction

Essence: fat is destroyed by the action of a heat ray. The removal of fat is not required, since it burns. Plus method: easy to carry, punctures in the skin are not visible after one day, does not require removal of stitches. Minus of the method: laser liposuction is suitable for solving small problem areas.

Recovery after surgery

  • Sutures are removed in a week, plus or minus two days.
  • Return to normal life rhythm can be in 3 weeks maximum.
  • Physical exercise, sports are allowed after 2 months.
  • At least one month should wear a compression underwear.
  • The final result after 3 months will be evident.

What determines the result of liposuction?

From the elastic properties of the skin. If the tone is weakened, it is necessary to do excision of excess skin. Often, liposuction is combined with abdominoplasty.
From strict adherence to recommendations after surgery. For example, the appointment of additional procedures, such as massage, lymphatic drainage.
From the patient’s lifestyle after surgery. A competent diet and reasonable distribution of physical activity will help to prevent weight gain.

What are the contraindications to the liposuction in Baltimore?

  • Diabetes.
  • Non-ideal coagulation of blood.
  • Pregnancy, as well as the period of breastfeeding.

Liposuction is recommended for:

  • For efficient and effective correction of the body.
  • For long-term effect after surgery.
  • To enhance self-esteem.
