Spokane Liposuction Cost And Before And After Photos

Liposuction of the abdomen in Spokane is often positioned as a universal, accessible and simple way of correcting the body shape.

There is a feeling that correction of appearance defects by surgical removal of surplus fatty tissue has no limitations. Even with obesity of 3 or 4 degree, you can return the wasp waist, you just need to register for a plastic surgeon.

What are the best candidates for liposuction?

The main question is: who can have a belly liposuction? Is it possible to do liposuction for obesity, or is the procedure for surgical correction of the body suitable only for people with a local surplus of body fat? The answer is not as simple as it seems.

Yes, on the one hand, liposuction is not a method of fighting obesity!

This is very important to understand. If the patient has obesity 3 or 4 degrees (to determine the degree of obesity is very simple even at home – it is enough to measure the waist circumference and compare the figures with the stages of obesity), surgical correction is not carried out.

With obesity of 1 or 2 degrees plastic surgeons can remove fatty deposits from problem areas, one of which is just the front wall of the abdomen, especially its lower part.

But in this case, too, the operation is aimed at correcting the body, correcting the local shortcomings of appearance, and not fighting with obesity.

After liposuction in the body will be a huge amount of subcutaneous fat (not less than 22lb), to cope with which will help solely diet and increased physical activity.

So, liposuction of the abdomen is indicated to patients with a local surplus of fatty tissue along the anterior abdominal wall.

Liposuction is performed with obesity of 1 or 2 degrees, but is not a method of fighting obesity, and from the point of view of correction of total body weight is ineffective.

Where else I can remove fat deposits? Popular zones of liposuction are the gluteal region, especially in the lower quadrants of the buttocks, the outer surface of the thigh, and the chin.

Plastic surgery will help to correct other problem areas, only adiposuction of the upper back, in the region of the knees, shin and the front surface of the thigh is contraindicated.

Warnings and rules of the recovery period:

  • It is strictly forbidden to exercise and lift weights.
  • You should not sunbathe in a tanning salon or under the sun.
  • It is necessary to refrain from alcohol and smoking.
  • You can not go to a sauna, take hot baths.
  • Swimming in the pool and natural water bodies is prohibited.

Strictly observing the instructions of the recovery period helps to speed up the healing and get the maximum aesthetic result. In addition, the implementation of all the recommendations of the doctor will avoid complications, including infection of the wound, inflammation, bleeding.

Preparation for surgery

The patient before lipoplasty undergoes a medical examination, including a standard list of tests. The purpose of the examination is to make sure there are no contraindications to liposuction.

The specific moment of preparation for abdominal liposuction is the production of individual compression underwear (belt), which must be worn at the stage of rehabilitation. A few weeks before the plastic need to stop smoking and give up alcohol. Do not take drugs that reduce blood clotting. It is also desirable for a while to stop taking any food additives.

List of necessary analyzes

A standard list of diagnostic procedures includes blood tests, an ECG, a test for viral hepatitis and HIV.

What are the contraindications for liposuction in Spokane?

Lipoplasty of the abdomen is contraindicated in persons with endocrinological pathology, oncological disease, heart and vascular diseases in the stage of decompensation. During pregnancy and breast-feeding, operations are not performed. The reason for the postponement of the procedure for a later period may be catarrhal disease, intestinal infection, rashes on the skin of the stomach, menstrual, fever of unknown origin.
