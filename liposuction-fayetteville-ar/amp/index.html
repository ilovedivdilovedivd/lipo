Liposuction In Fayetteville AR

In our progressive society, cosmetic surgery is becoming more affordable. Many modern women turn to the services of plastic surgeons as often as, say, to the dentist.

For some – cosmetic surgery has firmly entered the arsenal of mandatory measures to preserve beauty and youth. For example, fat was tried to scrape out from the subcutaneous fat curettes, which are designed to perform abortions.

And in the early seventies of the last century, almost simultaneously Kesselring found that the subcutaneous adipose tissue can be destroyed by a blunt tubule while sucking it through the same tube.

The method was called: fat suction, liposuction. Liposuction in Fayetteville, AR proved to be a very convenient method for eliminating local fat deposits.

On the human body, fat is distributed in a certain, far from accidental way.

Try to take a sheet of paper and draw a contour of a person, not just an abstract person, but, for example, a 16-year-old girl, next to a 50-year-old woman, or a man, and you’ll see the distribution of subcutaneous fat corresponds to the sex and age of a person.

But it does not always correspond to the canons of beauty accepted in society today.

Excessive fat deposits in the upper part of the lateral surface of the thigh – the “ears”, on the inner surface of the knee joints, on the waist are often prevented from choosing suitable clothing, contribute to the enhancement of the inferiority complex. In men, the most common problem is the abdomen.

Here and the surgeon comes to the rescue with liposuction. After removing excess fat, the skin above it, as a rule, shrinks and ceases to be superfluous. In young men, the skin is reduced better than in the elderly, in men it is better than in women.

It should be emphasized that a specific female problem – cellulite is not solved by liposuction. Skin condition does not improve this operation. It serves to correct the contours of the body.

Indications

Thus, the ideal candidate for liposuction is a young woman or a young man with severe local fat deposits.

However, gradually, as experience accumulates, the permissible amount of fat removed increases, and megaliposuction is already performed, when 15-20 liters of fat detritus are removed at a time.

These operations, of course, are potentially dangerous, and require specialized anesthesia in the course of the operation, and in the immediate postoperative period.

Complications

Like any surgical operation, liposuction in Fayetteville, AR can give a complication. The most catastrophic is a common inflammation, phlegmon of subcutaneous fat, often leading to death. Fortunately, the complication is very rare. In addition, there are skin irregularities due to uneven fat removal. These problems can be corrected.

Postoperative period

The peculiarity of liposuction is that the result of the operation is not immediately obvious. Already on the second day, the channels under the skin, which used to be fat, begin to fill with tissue fluid, and edema develops.

This swelling is so pronounced that it practically returns the patient to preoperative volumes. Reduction of swelling begins at week 3 after surgery, and a month later the result of the operation is visible. In the following months, the scar ripens in the subcutaneous tissue, as a result of which the skin is tightened, and the volume decreases still slightly. There is no need to take antibiotics.

Anesthesia

The often asked question is how much it hurts. The operation is painless, because an anesthesiologist works at the surgery. If liposuction is performed under local anesthesia, then the process of impregnating tissues with an anesthetic is unpleasant, sometimes painful.

Then there are no painful sensations. But after the operation there is pain. The operated zones ache a bit, completely this unpleasant sensation will disappear approximately in a month. Unpleasant sensations are smoothed out by wearing compression underwear.

Conclusion

Liposuction is a good operation, it gives a very good result, but the main condition, as for any operation, is that it must be performed according to the indications.
