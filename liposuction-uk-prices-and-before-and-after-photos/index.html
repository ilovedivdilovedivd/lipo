Liposuction United Kingdom Prices And Before And After Photos

Lipo in London, contrary to popular belief, is not a method of losing weight.

This is an operation that improves the proportions and shapes of body parts in people who do not always suffer from excessive weight.

It is prescribed only when fat surpluses are concentrated locally, for example, on the abdomen, back, hips. Such problems are very typical for citizens who lead a sedentary lifestyle.

Even people who regularly visit the gym, pool resort to correction of body parts. It is noticed that even fitness and rigid diets can not completely remove the protruding tummy and “ears” on the hips, and liposuction will cope with such problems.

In addition, the result after correction remains for a long time, and if all the prescriptions are observed, you can forget about the excess fat for a couple of years.

All types of surgery have no special contraindications and are considered relatively safe. Of course, this procedure can not be used by people suffering from diabetes, somatic and infectious diseases, as well as in case of poor blood clotting.

The result is finally visible only after a few months.

Areas of application of liposuction

Abdomen

Probably, every third woman would not refuse to make a correction of the abdomen. This procedure is the most popular and popular with lovely ladies. And this is not accidental. To date, this is the most effective and less painful way to get rid of the excess fat layer in this area of the body.

Bribes and the fact that the consequences in the form of complications are revealed extremely rarely, while the result is obvious and persists for several years. This procedure is recommended for people who have local fatty clumps in the abdomen.

This leads to breaking and erasing the clear contours of the figure. It is important to note that this problem practically does not disappear even after exercise and rigid diets.

Despite the fact that the cost of the operation is not low, it still occupies a leading position in aesthetic surgery. First, a problem such as a rounded protruding belly is observed very often in both men and women, even at a young age.

Secondly, the procedure itself is relatively uncomplicated and at the same time gives excellent noticeable and persistent results. Third, after the operation, only barely noticeable scars remain.

The price for correction of the abdomen will depend on the complexity of the operation and the number of problem areas.

Thighs

Some women have a genetic predisposition to the formation of “ears on the hips.” In this case, neither diet nor exercise this feature of the body will not adjust. There is only liposuction. With this procedure, you can remove excess fat from both the external and internal surfaces of the thigh.

It is noticed that many women, even without excess weight, are genetically predisposed to fatty clusters in the upper part of the thigh. On its outer side, so-called “ears” are often formed. This greatly spoils the figure.

At the same time, it is almost impossible to hide this problem with clothing. Excess fat in this area, too, can not be removed by exercise or diet. The correction of the “ears” helps to cope with this problem, the deposition of adipose tissue in this zone will disappear as a result of the procedure.

Legs will look slimmer, and thighs much narrower. Liposuction of the inner thigh is indicated to women with excess weight, who, when walking, thigh rub against each other.

This causes severe discomfort. In this area, a large volume of adipose tissue can not be removed, since the skin in this zone has a weak contractility.

Therefore, the procedure of “smoothing” liposuction is performed. The risk of unevenness is minimized. The cost of this operation will depend on the level of complexity and number of problem areas.
