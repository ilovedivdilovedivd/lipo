Totally! Cosmetic filler for around the mouth!
Mar 6, 2018
Morning! It’s totally possible to use dermal fillers to erase the lines around your mouth (and contour the chin!) We’d recommend Volbella or vollure for that area because those products have smaller molecules and offer flexibility so it will look much more natural than a thicker product!

Do you recommend Voluma for lips if I want a natural looking and feeling pout?
No Voluma in Lips!
Mar 6, 2018
Okay, so maybe that was an aggressive answer title, but Voluma is a VERY LARGE molecule and looks best in cheeks and chins. For lips you’re gonna want to stick with Juvederm, Restylane, Volbella or Vollure! These are more flexible and smaller molecules that will help you look more natural and pout like.
