How long would I have to wait in between of having two different surgeries, by two differ
A few months from one another should be enough.
Feb 12, 2018
Since you are treating different areas, there is little effect one surgery will have over the other. However, it could have some effects over the recovery process of the first surgery, which may cause more noticeable scars. The most optimal course of action would be having both at the same time, but since that is not possible, a month or two should suffice.

Staying smoke free several months before the surgery will definitely improve the healing process, but if you already come from several decades of smoking habits, do not expect to have the same healing quality as a person who hasn’t ever smoked or even a person who is only occasionally exposed to second hand smoking.
