Liposuction In Corpus Christi TX

Liposuction in Corpus Christi is an operation that is designed to eliminate local fat deposits. In the world of plastic surgery and aesthetic medicine, it has been used for many years, so today it is very well studied and, therefore, safe technology.

Do not forget that liposuction of the body is not a cure for obesity. The technique is aimed at improving the contours and appearance of the body.

In each individual case, the program of procedure may differ. For example, when removing fat deposits from a body area on which there is an excessive amount of skin, the operation is combined with abdominoplasty.

The procedure is performed using a vacuum device, suction fat, steel tubes-cannula and other tools.

The essence of the operation is more eloquently stated by its second name – liposculpture. Having eliminated the necessary amount of excess fat, you can form an attractive silhouette, regain lost slenderness or adjust proportions.

The operation can be carried out on almost all parts of the body, but it shows the most striking effect in the region of the hips, abdomen, calves, waist, chin, arms and back – that is, in areas of local fat deposits.

Laser liposuction in Corpus Christi is one of the types of surgery.

There are several types of surgeries to remove excess fat:

  • Laser;
  • Ultrasound;
  • Tumescent.

Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages. For example, ultrasonic liposuction allows to do without surgical intervention. It is practically painless, but its cost can be called quite high. In fact, during the procedure, ultrasound is affected by fat deposits, which destroys fatty tissues, turning them into water and fatty acids that are gradually eliminated from the body.

Popular techniques for liposuction

In plastic surgery, this procedure has been practiced for a long time, which affected the number of existing techniques. As a rule, the liposuction of the body in our clinic is carried out using several techniques:

1. The syringe method

It is based on pumping out the lipid tissue with a syringe. This is the least traumatic liposuction, which guarantees the absence of complications. But it is suitable for eliminating small amounts of adipose tissue, usually in the chin area, around the knees, so on.

2. Standard liposuction

Excess fat deposits are evacuated by vacuum suction. This method is the most popular and widespread. Before the procedure, a special solution is introduced into the treatment zone. Further on the body the doctor makes incisions, and in them immerses the cannula, which are connected with vacuum suction. A negative pressure is created, due to which the fat is pumped out.

3. Tumescent liposuction. The doctor treats the area of exposure with special drugs that destroy the structure of adipose tissue. Further, the ground fat is sucked off with a vacuum tool.

4. Ultrasonic liposuction

Before pumping out, the fat cells are destroyed by ultrasonic waves. The main advantage of this technique is its low traumatism: during the procedure cannulas of small diameter are used, which minimizes skin damage.

Recovery period

The recovery period depends entirely on the volume of the operation. If a patient needs to get rid of a large volume of lipid tissue, then he must be ready that the postoperative recovery will be accompanied by moderate pain, swelling.

Also, among post-operation symptoms, there may be an increase in temperature, subcutaneous hemorrhage, and a decrease in sensitivity in the area of the operation.

Wearing compression underwear and adherence to the regime established by the doctor are important components of successful and rapid rehabilitation. Normalization of the skin, restoration of its elasticity can take up to six months.
