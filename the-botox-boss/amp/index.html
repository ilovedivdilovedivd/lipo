How Long Does Botox Last?
Botox results last…
Aug 31, 2018
Botox is a muscular injection that is metabolized by the body. You can expect your results to last about 3-4 months (patients have reported results lasting anywhere between 2-6 months). Some things that can affect the duration of your results are: metabolism, correct dosing, the strength and activeness of the site treated, and appropriate care & handling of the product before being injected.
