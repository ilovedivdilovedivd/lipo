Liposuction Cincinnati Prices

Liposuction in Cincinnati is a complex of actions aimed at eliminating the fat layer. According to the type of target effect, liposuction is divided into non-surgical and – conducted through surgical intervention.

The cost of performing all the surgical liposuction procedures depends on the scales and the number of treated zones, and also on the method used.

Causes

– Liposuction of the abdomen and waist in Cincinnati allows you to significantly reduce the waist – remove the “hanging sides” and “shaking belly.”
– Thighs – corrects shapes and sizes, giving the thighs slender and aesthetics (gets rid of cellulite).

– The cheek and neck are a rather delicate procedure that allows you to remove the puffiness of the face and the “second chin”.

– Forearms and hands – removes the visual massiveness, leaving the natural forms due to skeletal muscle.

Types

Vacuum liposuction in Cincinnati occurs by inserting the cannula under the skin at the site of the local effect (which is true for all methods of operations for the destruction / removal of fat cells) and the translational motion of the cannulae.

Removal of fat, as in all methods of surgical intervention, occurs through a vacuum device.

Ultrasonic liposuction is considered a risky way to affect fat cells, since ultrasonic waves are highly likely to cause burns of internal organs. It is forbidden in a number of countries, including the USA. With electronic liposuction, an alternating electric current is used as the destructive factor.

Laser liposuction effectively destroys fatty layers, but requires a lot of experience of the surgeon, maximum caution and accuracy in movements.

Waterjet lipouction BodyJet provides the necessary destructive effect by means of a thin water jet, under high pressure applied to the fatty deposits.

The main differences between non-surgical and surgical liposuction are the methods of destructive effect / fat removal, as well as the long-term effect achieved.

Non-surgical liposuction is performed during several procedures for a fairly long time; The results, obtained in the end are not final, since fatty tissues are destroyed, but are not removed (the destroyed fat cells are excreted by the body on their own).

The surgery, performed in the framework of surgical liposuction, lasts from thirty minutes to one and a half hours. During the operation, the fat layer is destroyed by one of the selected techniques with complete removal of the fat cells through the cannula.

Contraindications

  • Dangerous physical illnesses;
  • Pathology / heart disease;
  • diabetes;
  • pregnancy;
  • mental disorders;
  • Oncological pathologies;
  • hypertension.

Features

  • The amount of fat removed depends on the method used and the specific medical indications. In one operation, a maximum of 0.79-1.58 gallons is removed. The duration of the operation is from thirty minutes to an hour and a half and depends on the volume removed.
  • Depending on the technique used and the amount of fat removed, liposuction is performed under local or general anesthesia.
  • Stitches are removed one week after the operation.
  • Required stay in the clinic for one day.

To their professional duties after the operation can begin in 3-14 days.

Recovery

For up to six weeks after liposuction in areas exposed to operational impacts, as a rule, there is a swelling; Possible hematomas. Postoperative recovery is performed to eliminate possible complications and increase the target effect, which can last for eight weeks.

The number of rehabilitation actions includes:

  • wear a special compression underwear;
  • Physiotherapy and massage;
  • Restriction of physical activity, in connection with which you can resume sports only after the completion of recovery.

In the postoperative period, a gradual increase in the positive effect occurs, which is enhanced by a decrease in swelling and the removal of destroyed fatty tissues.

Therefore, the result “before and after” liposuction will be most revealing, six months after the operation.

On comparative photos and videos, you can evaluate the changes, that have taken place. Photos and videos that emphasize the difference before and after, are the best argument in favor of surgical liposuction. In this case, only the doctor should determine the appropriateness of these measures.
