Liposuction Fremont CA

Liposuction in Fremont is a method of getting rid of excess fat deposits. Many women mistakenly believe that liposuction will relieve not only of excess pounds, but also of cellulite.

But this is not so, liposuction only helps to correct and reduce the necessary parts of the body.

The younger the patient, the brighter the result, faster healing, since at the age of 40 years the skin is still elastic and is quickly restored after the operation.

There are several types of liposuction in Fremont

1. Liposuction is also divided into two types: wet and dry. The latter type is not popular because of the huge number of complications, besides, dry liposuction is quite traumatic.

But, the principle of these two species is one, with the help of vacuum suction, cannulas – very thin needles, eliminate local fat deposits.

The cannula is injected under negative pressure through a small incision on the skin. Dry liposuction differs from wet liposuction in that solutions that contain anesthetic and other medications that contribute to reducing bleeding and facilitate the removal of fatty deposits are not injected into the treated area.

For one procedure the surgeon can remove 2lb-4lb of fat, while removing more fat can cause serious damage to human health. After the operation, the places of incisions are sewn up, a very tight elastic bandage or bandage is applied to the operated area.

The next day after the operation, you need to wear compression underwear, and you will need to wear it for one to two months. Both types of liposuction are performed under general anesthesia, you can leave the hospital after surgery only after three days.
After a session of liposuction, infiltrating fluid will flow through the incisions on the skin. The sutures dissolve in a week on their own, but you can not wash your week, you will have to wipe your body with a damp towel, avoiding the operated area. About the solarium, swimming pool and gym for a month will have to forget.

Repeated liposuction session can be performed only after six months. The duration of the operation is from half an hour to two hours, and after the end of the anesthesia, you can feel quite a lot of pain. There may also be the appearance of bruising and swelling. The shortcomings of this procedure include the appearance of cavities and hillocks after pumping out fat.

The very first result of the operation can be seen in a month and a half.

2. Syringe liposuction.

This type of liposuction is used to pump fat in small areas, for example, on the knees, in the area of the fatty hump on the back, and also on the chin. The operation is performed as follows: in places of fat accumulation the doctor inserts under the skin a needle, begins to pull the piston, thereby creating a negative pressure.

Syringe liposuction very accurate, precise and less traumatic operation than in the first case. The duration of this operation is about thirty minutes, there are few swelling and bruises, there are practically no complications. The result of syringe liposuction can be seen in a month.

3. Ultrasound liposuction.

This operation is that the fat deposits are broken up to the liquid state with the help of ultrasound, and then removed by titanium cannulas with the help of vacuum suction. The skin with this type of liposuction is minimally injured, and the skin under the influence of ultrasound is better reduced, which provides a more even surface after the operation. The procedure is performed under local or general anesthesia.

The recovery period is rather short, the patient is in a hospital all day. For two months after ultrasonic liposuction, compression underwear should be worn. The duration of the operation is about two hours, and a noticeable result can be seen at the end of the second week.

The disadvantage of ultrasound surgery is the accumulation of tissue fluid in the operated sites.

4. Tumescent liposuction in Fremont

This type of operation does not require general anesthesia.

The operation is performed as follows: a special solution consisting of adrenaline, ice-caffeine and saline solution is introduced into the problem zone with the help of a syringe, the amount of which must twice exceed the amount of fat processed. Then begins the destruction of fat and its aspiration. With tumescent liposuction, blood loss and trauma are less than, for example, with classical liposuction. The patient after surgery performs one night in the hospital, and the postoperative period is very short. Within two weeks, you will need to wear special compression underwear. The result of this liposuction can be seen only after four months.

5. Non-invasive (non-surgical) ultrasound operation is the leader. Using this special Ultra Shape Contour I, this type of operation is performed.

During the procedure, ultrasound only affects fat cells. The operation does not require anesthesia. The procedure is practically painless, only warmth and tingling can be felt, so patients often sleep during the procedure. The operation consists in the fact that ultrasonic pulses act on the problem zones, destroying, at the same time, the fat cells cage and their molecules immediately enter the blood and are eliminated naturally within three weeks. In one session, you can get rid of 1 lb of fat. The skin remains flat, no swelling, no bruising. In the first week, it is recommended to limit the consumption of fatty foods, this will allow the body to quickly remove fat cells.

Ultra Shape Contour I with great accuracy calculates the area of influence and ultrasound does not hit twice in the same place. The operation can take up to three hours and is considered the safest for the patient.

Regardless of what kind of liposuction you prefer, these operations have their contraindications: violations of kidney and liver function, pregnancy, cardiovascular diseases, diabetes mellitus, oncology, hypertension, propensity to form blood clots, low blood coagulability. Non-surgical liposuction also has a number of contraindications: a stomach hernia, hepatitis, renal failure, a divergence of the rectus abdominis muscles, prosthetic knee and hip joints, immune system diseases.
