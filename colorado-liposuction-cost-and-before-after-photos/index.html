What is Liposuction?

The uniqueness of this method lies in the fact that only with its help can fat be removed as quickly as possible in the most unsightly places on the body.

Non-surgical methods of losing weight against the background of liposuction are ineffective.

When a person tries diets, exposes his body to physical exertion, massages and takes pills, he can achieve only a temporary effect. In the case of liposuction, the result achieved remains forever.

In view of the fact, that this procedure is carried out by highly qualified specialists, after a very short period of time you will be able to find a slender body and return to the ordinary way of life.

Having a slim body shape and tightened tummy, you will become more confident in yourself and be able to afford what you previously refused because of your complexes.

Features of liposuction

One of the important advantages of liposuction in Colorado is that along with the absence of complications, this procedure does not pose any threat to human health. It does not provide an incisions, which precludes the appearance of scarring on the skin after surgery.

Chin Liposuction Procedure With Dr Andrew Wolfe, Colorado Plastic Surgeon

The basis for this procedure for fat removal is the creation of small punctures in combination with general anesthesia. Usually this procedure takes about 30-60 minutes.

However, in some cases, the duration of the operation can be increased. This may depend on the area of the treated zone and the volume of fat deposits. For example, to remove fat from the stomach, it takes about 30-40 minutes.

The method is based on the effect on fat, which allows it to give a liquid consistency. After that, a special vacuum device is used and they are pumped out through the thin cannula tubes.

In different Colorado clinics they use their devices for liposuction. The main difference between them lies in the technology used, which allows to destroy and provide fragmentation of adipose tissue.

Such high-temperature methods of liposuction, as laser, ultrasound and others can not be considered painless, because when they occur, there is a danger of complications in the form of skin burns and internal organs.

All this forced specialists in the field of plastic surgery to switch to using water-jet liposuction in Colorado. This unique method became available thanks to the German medical scientists who invented it.

The high safety of this liposuction is not its only advantage. Among the positive qualities should be attributed and high productivity: only one procedure is enough to pump out from the treated area up to 1.58 gallon of fat.

In addition to this, the use of water-jet liposuction of the abdomen allows to give the skin in this area an ideal surface. At the same time, the slightest irregularities of the skin are excluded here.
