650 – 450cc, breast made smaller/implant exchange disaster! Thoughts?
Re: exchange disaster
Sep 21, 2023
When I exchange implants and decrease in size this much, I always talk with the patient about a lift as well. The skin envelope needs to be made smaller, or it will be redundant and the implant won’t fill it out as well as the previous one did. Of course this would require scars and some patients are unwilling to proceed with the lift for that reason.

I currently vape, have upcoming breast mastopexy + Aug Oct 18,2023- questions About nicotine
Question re: Vaping
Sep 12, 2023
In my practice I prefer for patients to be nicotine-free 6 weeks prior to surgery and 6 weeks after. I usually get urine cotinine testing at pre-op visit to ensure there are no further nicotine metabolites in the system! Vapes are unregulated and thus even vapes advertised as nicotine-free can be problematic in that some of them do include nicotine (evidenced on positive cotinine testing some of my patients). I would recommend avoiding all vapes 6 weeks before and 6 weeks after for best healing.
