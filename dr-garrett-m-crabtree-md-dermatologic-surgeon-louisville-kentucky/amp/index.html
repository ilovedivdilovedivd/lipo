Feb 20, 2019
You can have liposuction on consecutive days but the main concern is the amount of lidocaine used. You will still have some in your system from the previous day. If a large amount was used the previous day , it might be better to wait longer than a day for a second procedure. This is something that you should discuss with your surgeon. Dr Crabtree
