Would a mini tummy tuck with liposuction correct my diastasis recti or will I need a full tummy tuck?
Diastasis Repair
Oct 24, 2023
A mini tummy tuck will not address the separation of the abdominal muscles. For a true diastasis repair, a full abdominoplasty will be required.
Why did my diastasis recti' repair not hold up? Will it happen again after the 2nd repair?
Diastasis repair failure
Jan 14, 2022
It is not very common for a properly repaired diastasis to come undone. When it happens it is usually early in the post-operative period from increased intra-abdominal pressure (such as coughing or vomiting). Sometimes, patients will mistake a recurrence of the diastasis for visceral fat (fat underneath the muscle).

Hyperhydrosis is excessive sweating in multiple areas. For hyperhydrosis, neurotoxin is a game changer and decreases the sweating of the most common places such as palms and underarms.
