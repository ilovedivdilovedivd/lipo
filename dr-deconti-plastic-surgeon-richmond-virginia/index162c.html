What is right for me generally to fix my saggy breasts?
Breast lift or mastopexy for saggy breasts.
Mar 3, 2021
A breast lift, or mastopexy, is the best option to lift your sagging breasts and decrease the areola giving you a firmer more perky breast. If you like the size of the breasts in a bra, then you only need the lift. If you would like to be smaller then a reduction would be performed. A breast reduction is basically a breast lift with the removal of breast tissue.
I hope this helps.Best wishes,Dr. D.
Is it OK to get dermal fillers despite having an upper motor neuron facial nucleus lesion? (Photo
Dermal fillers
Mar 3, 2021
There is no contraindication to getting dermal fillers in a patient with a n upper motor neuron facial nucleus lesion. The fillers can help balance the facial asymmetry and help the patient feel better about themselves. I do not recommend getting Botox though. I would recommend getting a medical release from your neurologist about the fillers. This may help the injector feel better about doing it from a medical legal point of view.
I hope this helps.Best wishes,Dr. D.
Can you use Hydroquinone after a TCA peel?
Hydroquinone after a TCA peel.
Mar 2, 2021
You can use hydroquinone after a TCA peel when the skin has fully healed. Many times I will start a patient on hydroquinone after a peel to prevent hyperpigmentation. I recommend checking with the physician who performed your peel first.

Firm breast implants.

What kind of anesthesia is the norm for labiaplasty?
Types of anesthesia used for a labiaplasty.
Feb 23, 2021
The most common types of anesthesia used for a labiaplasty are IV sedation and general anesthesia. I use general anesthesia so the patient is very comfortable. I do not recommend doing it under just local. The procedure can be very uncomfortable and painful depending on what is done. I would recommend you wait until 4 months after you have finished breastfeeding before having this procedure.
I read chromic sutures are bad for a labiaplasty. Is that true?
Chromic sutures for a labiaplasty.
Feb 23, 2021
It is perfectly acceptable to use chromic sutures for a labiaplasty. Most surgeons will use some form of absorbale suture for the closure. The most common are Monocryl, Chromic, or Vicryl. They are typically left in place for 3-4 weeks.
I hope this helps.Best wishes,Dr. D.
