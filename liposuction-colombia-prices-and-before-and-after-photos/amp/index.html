Liposuction Colombia Prices And Before And After Photos

Liposuction in Colombia is a procedure that leads to the removal of unwanted fat deposits in some parts of the body.

It is especially recommended to perform liposuction in places where fat can not be removed even with intense physical exertion or diets.

Liposuction has long been considered one of the most effective ways to remove fat and to date. Many plastic surgery clinics offer this service.

And there are life situations when you need to look at all 100 points. You may need a loan because liposuction is not a cheap procedure and requires a certain financial base.

Liposuction is performed as an independent procedure, and in conjunction with some cosmetic manipulations.

Most often this operation is to remove fat on the abdomen, chin, inside of the thighs, on the buttocks, back, in the chest area.

After liposuction, the contour of the body improves due to the direct removal of fat deposits. The ideal result is seen in patients whose skin is elastic.

Contraindications

Some clinics in the pursuit of big money neglect the rules of plastic surgery and do not take into account all factors, do not evaluate the patient’s health condition and possible contraindications.

And their list is quite impressive! Liposuction is not carried out with small amount of subcutaneous fat, a good decent surgeon with a positive reputation will offer you a hardware cosmetology or gym, offer you a diet, but not an operation with anesthesia.

Layer of fat under the skin is necessary for a person to absorb, heat and beauty the body, smoothing the body’s reliefs – good doctors know about it. But if the doctor will give in to the entreaties of the operation in such cases is usually disappointing, other procedures could be more useful.

In addition, liposuction is prohibited at high blood pressure and disorders, with kidney and liver problems, flabbiness and poor elasticity of the skin.

Dangers of the operation

In addition to the rapid return of extra pounds, with no changes in lifestyle, surgery to remove excess fat can cause many more side effects.

This, above all, a violation of the sensitivity of the skin in the area of surgery, which is due to damage to nerve endings and swelling of the subcutaneous tissue.

This unpleasant symptom can persist for a long time. On the skin in the field of operation there may be a long time not passing bruises and pigment accumulations, the skin in the area of incisions can become infected.

Do not forget about the shock from the introduction of anesthesia, which is in one case a million operations, but is dangerous right up to a lethal outcome.

Another fatal complication may be damage to a large vessel and the development of fatty thromboembolism – getting into the lumen of the fat vessel and blockage of large blood vessels.

Because of the loss of a sufficiently large volume of blood, postoperative anemia can develop – it significantly impairs the patient’s well-being.

Enjoy the full success of the operation you can not earlier than four months. During this time, everything will finally heal, the tissues will be tightened, and the body will come to form. Until that time, recovery will be necessary with the wearing of special uderwear, refusal to visit saunas and baths, limiting physical exertion.
