Liposuction Pittsburgh Prices & Photos

Liposuction is one of the most effective and efficient methods of plastic correction of body contours due to elimination of local fat deposits.

In Pittsburgh clinic, this procedure is one of the most popular after a plastic breast enlargement surgery.

In fact, this is a safe and simple operation, which requires only tiny puncture of the skin, performed under general anesthesia.

The duration of the operation does not take more than 60-90 minutes, but the effect is amazing – for this time our specialists manage to rid your body of 8lb-11lb of hated fat from problem areas.

Types of liposuction

Laser liposuction

Laser liposuction is also called sculptural plastic, because it helps to literally “fashion” the ideal body that the patient dreams of.

This procedure is suitable not only for people with excess weight, but also slender, because no one is immune from the appearance of problem areas.

Abdominal liposuction

The greatest effect can be obtained only if there are pronounced fat deposits. Much attention is paid to the lateral areas, as well as the condition of the skin and muscle tissue.

To achieve the necessary parameters of the torso, we apply commensurate planning of removal of fat from several areas at the same time: from the zone of funk, shoulders, arms, abdomen and side areas of the waist.

Liposuction of the chin

As a rule, in this area the removal of fat as a separate operation is extremely rare. We recommend this procedure only in conjunction with a facelift and consider liposuction of the face, neck and chin as one of the stages of facial and neck plastic surgery.

Indications for liposuction

The greatest visual effectiveness of liposuction is noticeable in patients with pronounced fat deposits, therefore any local formation of fatty tissue in such places of the human body as: lower and upper limbs, back, face, hips, chest, waist can become indications.

The best candidates for the procedure are patients from 18 years old, whose weight exceeds the ideal body weight by 30%. But patients from the age of 40 old can be shown plastic surgery to tighten the skin due to lack of (age) muscle tone and less dense and less elastic skin.

Types of liposuction in Pittsburgh

Vacuum liposuction is a traditional technique carried out using cannulae (small tubes) through small incisions;
Ultrasonic liposuction is the destruction of the fat layer due to the action of ultrasonic waves, it is also excreted through the cannula.

Radiofrequency or electronic liposuction is used to destroy fatty tissue using the properties of alternating current. The removal of the destroyed fatty tissue is carried out with the help of cannulas;

Laser liposuction. Here our specialists use modern laser technology and a vacuum technique for removing adipose tissue;

Water Jet Liposuction BodyJet – this technique involves the use of a thin stream of water under high pressure and vacuum suction. For today it is the most advanced, the most safe technology of plastic correction of the body.

The preparatory period before the liposuction procedure involves collecting information about the patient, so you need to provide information about all diseases of your body (if any), about existing allergies to medicines, about the use of medicines, vitamins and dietary supplements at the time, as well as information on surgeries, exposure to bad habits (smoking, alcohol).

During the preparatory period, you need to have several tests:

  • general urine test;
  • Result of biochemical blood test;
  • The results of blood tests for venereal diseases;
  • ECG.

Immediately 6-8 hours before the procedure you are required to refuse to eat food and alcohol, and follow the recommendations that were provided by our specialist at the preliminary reception.

Recovery after liposuction

Postoperative recovery lasts 2-3 weeks, during which it is necessary to abandon sports exercises and high physical exertion. In the operated areas we impose compression underwear, wearing it for 30 or more days can significantly reduce swelling and helps the skin integrate “get used” to a new form.

During the recovery period, you should not visit the sauna and solarium, it is recommended to revise your daily diet and include healthy meals. Daily walks in the open air help to survive the rehabilitation period painlessly.

Contraindications

People who suffer from the following problems can not use the liposuction procedure:

  • Pronounced overweight (obese). Liposuction can not be a means of combating obesity, such patients, first of all, need to contact endocrinologists and nutritionists. And only after weight adjustment in this way can you turn to liposuction for rapid removal of fatty deposits;
  • Diabetes and endocrine diseases;
  • Diseases of the blood and lungs;
  • Mental disorders;
  • Any physical illness in severe form;
  • Infectious diseases in acute form.

It is also not recommended to perform a repeat operation in areas where liposuction has already been done.
