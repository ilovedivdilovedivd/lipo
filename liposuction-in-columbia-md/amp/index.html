Liposuction In Columbia MD

Liposuction in Columbia, MD is a surgical operation to remove excess adipose tissue and tighten sagging skin to give the figure elegance and harmony.

The body accumulates fat to protect against cold, injuries and in case of starvation.

Fat cells are located very deep in the skin, so physical activity, dieting significantly reduce their number and volume can not.

In addition, they are able to recover from reserve cells, located just above the pubic area.

To prevent this from happening, liposuction is done, including in the abdomen.

Indication for the operation:

  • a very large layer of fat on the stomach, which does not go away with diet, exercise, proper nutrition;
  • pronounced, sagging folds of fat;
  • large stretch marks;
  • postoperative scars.

Sequence of the operation:

  1. The places of incisions and zones of liposuction are marked (they do this in a standing position, as when tilted or lying, the skin and fat are shifted).
  2. General anesthesia is performed.
  3. Small incisions of the skin are made in the area of the pubis, navel or in natural folds.
  4. Adipose tissue is impregnated with tumescent fluid, which is a saline solution with lidocaine and adrenaline. After 30-40 minutes there is a strong swelling of the adipose tissue, the blood vessels contract, which prevents a strong loss of blood.
  5. Fat is removed through the incisions with a special needle. It is important to remember that during surgery with the stomach, you can remove only 200-2500 ml of fat.
  6. Stitches are applied.
  7. After the operation immediately compression garment is worn.

Adipose tissue can be processed by ultrasound, for this purpose emitters are used, which are introduced into small incisions in the skin of the abdomen.

As a result, the fat cells are liquefied and well sucked off with a vacuum device without any particular consequences.

Advantages of liposuction in Columbia, MD:

  • inconspicuous incisions in the skin;
  • operation is of low traumatic nature;
  • the body is quickly restored;
  • fat can be removed immediately from several problem areas;
  • After a short time, you can do repeated operations;
  • Liposuction can be combined with another operation, it does not affect human health.

Disadvantages of abdominal liposuction:

the result of the procedure is visible only after 1.5 months, an objective assessment is made after six months;

  • there may be some skin sagging. This is due to the fact that in elderly women the skin is inelastic, it loses its ability to contract. Excess skin is removed during surgery, but it can quickly sag again. The form will be adjusted, but the silhouette can be spoiled;
  • in fact, liposuction does not reduce body weight, remove only excess fat tissue in the abdomen;
  • If there are very large irregularities and severe flabbiness of the skin, liposuction is not recommended;
  • the procedure is quite expensive.

Contraindications to liposuction of the abdomen:

  • cardiac, pulmonary or renal failure;
  • diabetes, cancer, serious diseases of internal organs;
  • with obesity of a high degree, liposuction is ineffective;
  • blood coagulability;
  • patient age of 18 years;
  • present or planned during the year pregnancy.

It should be remembered that liposuction can be carried out only on a great need. Before the procedure, it is necessary to conduct a preliminary examination in order to avoid complications, choose a good clinic and an experienced plastic surgeon.

The cost of liposuction of the abdomen will be called by the doctor after a preliminary consultation.

After the liposuction of the abdomen, it is necessary to strictly follow all the doctor’s recommendations regarding the diet and exercise to quickly come back to normal and keep the results.
