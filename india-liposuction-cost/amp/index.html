How is liposuction performed?

Before proceeding to liposuction, the clinic doctor examines the patient and decides how this cosmetic procedure will be performed. The technique of liposuction is caused by many factors.

In the event that a fairly extensive operation is prescribed, in which three or more parts of the body are to be treated, general anesthesia is used. Immediately after surgery, patients should:
