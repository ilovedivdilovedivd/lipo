It is important that carrying out liposuction almost never causes complications and has a very small amount of contraindications.

In addition, those who underwent liposuction, note its nontraumatism, short recovery period, as well as the possibility of removing excess fatty tissue not from one problem zone, but from several.

As a rule, in one operation doctors can remove to 1.32 gallon adipose tissue.

Very often unscrupulous surgeons advertise liposuction as a lunch time procedure (outpatient procedure)! Or for one procedure they promise to remove up to 3.17 gallon of fat. It is not true.

Liposuction is, above all, an operation. Removing more than 5 liters of fat is accompanied by blood loss to 30-40%. Therefore, it is important to be confident in the surgeon and not to neglect the observation in the hospital after liposuction.

Skin incisions during liposuction are small, scarring after them does not remain – except that a small traces reminiscent of a scratch, which also disappears with time.

However, since liposuction is still a surgical intervention, liposuction should be attended to by choosing a reliable liposuction clinic in San Francisco and, of course, to consult a doctor about possible contraindications. Contraindications to liposuction are few, but nevertheless they are. In particular, with enlarged veins, liposuction is contraindicated. The whole truth about liposuction can be told only by a professional and only he can make a conclusion about whether this procedure is right for you, and also what type of liposuction is better to choose in this case.

Currently, there are many centers for liposuction in San Francisco, and the question of where to make liposuction, should be addressed very carefully. You can not economize on one’s own health, one can not agree to attractive advertising promises at first glance: it is necessary to choose exactly the clinic where liposuction is performed in a qualitative way. Prerequisites: a license and good patient feedback on liposuction.

Liposuction zones

Most plastic surgeons agree that liposuction can be done in almost any zone. Liposuction of the body and liposuction of the face will help remove excess fat and make your appearance almost ideal.

Liposuction of the abdomen

This is one of the most popular areas of liposuction in both men and women. However, not always after liposuction, the abdomen becomes flat – it’s not just about fat deposits, but also in stretching the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall. Therefore, the best results come from the combination of liposuction and abdominalplasty.

Liposuction of knees

Before deciding on the question of liposuction of the knees, the doctor will determine whether the problem areas are fat deposits or is a feature of the structure of the muscles or bones. If the case is in adipose tissue, then knee liposuction gives good results, especially if you combine liposuction with the popliteal areas.

Liposuction thighs

The topic that occupies the top lines of the “hit parades” of appeals to the clinic is liposuction of the thighs, that is, the external surface of the thigh. Liposuction of the inner thigh is also very popular.

In both cases of liposuction on the thigh, the doctor will necessarily check the elasticity of the skin – if it is deficient in addition to liposuction of the thigh inside or outside, a plastic surgery may also be required to remove excess skin.

Liposuction of legs

The main areas of liposuction are the legs and calves. Liposuction of calves is carried out in cases, when an elongated fatty cord is formed in this zone. Liposuction of the legs and liposuction of the calves makes the legs more elegant and slim – is not this an eternal female dream?

Liposuction of the back, sides and buttocks

Liposuction of the back allows you to remove ugly fatty deposits in both the scapula and the lumbar region. Good results are provided by liposuction on the back cushions: their removal makes the back even, and the body slender.

Liposuction of the sides, as a rule, is combined with liposuction of the hips, back, buttocks and helps to give the blurred body a young outlines.

Liposuction of the buttocks is also very popular, but from a technical point of view it is one of the most difficult areas – in fact here it is important not only to remove excess fat, but also not to make appetizing forms excessively flat.

Liposuction shoulder and arm

Elegant hands are one of the most obvious signs of youth. Not everyone notices how the upper part of the arms hang with age, but the result of liposuction of the hands can not be noticed. Even more beautiful is the shoulder line after performing neck liposuction.

Breast Liposuction

Typically, liposuction and breast plastic surgery are done simultaneously. A combination of breast enlargement surgery with liposuction, which removes excess fat tissue in the area around the bust, gives the figure a practically sculptural outline.

Liposuction of the chin

Another very popular in both women and men is the liposuction zone. However, liposuction of the double chin is most often performed not by itself, but together with liposuction of the face and facelift. After liposuction of the chin, cheek liposuction and facelift, not only excess fat is removed, but the skin condition improves, the face looks much younger.

Types of liposuction

Plastic surgery goes ahead in leaps and bounds, new ways of liposuction push the old, and in order to decide whether to do liposuction and which, the patient needs to have some knowledge about all the major types of liposuction. This allows patients in conjunction with the doctor to choose the safest method of liposuction in each case.

The main methods of liposuction, which the San Francisco clinic offers to date, are the following:

  • Tumescent liposuction,
  • Vacuum liposuction,
  • Non-surgical liposuction,
  • Ultrasonic liposuction,
  • Laser liposuction.

Do not forget, that to do liposuction enough once to forget about the problem once and for all.

The specialists of the clinic perform liposuction of any zone, choosing the ideal method of liposuction just for you.
