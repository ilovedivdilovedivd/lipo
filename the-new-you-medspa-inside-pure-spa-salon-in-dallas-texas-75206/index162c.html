Dr. Jeff Taylor

Name: Dr. Jeff Taylor
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 20
Biography: A board certified physician, Dr. Jeff Taylor established The New You Aesthetic Medicine Med Spa in 2007 after seven years of practicing at his own internal medicine family practice in Garland, Texas. Dr. Taylor discovered his passion for cosmetic medicine early in his practice and spent the next few years seeking the most thorough training available on the newest, most innovative procedures in the cosmetic medicine market. His interest in a more holistic view of medicine and emphasis on the importance of one’s self esteem and general well being, led him to further study aesthetic medicine. Following an excellent response from his patients receiving the new cosmetic treatments offered through his practice, Dr. Taylor sought an alternate venue to provide his patients with the ultimate care in medical grade skincare treatments in a spa-like atmosphere.
