Liposuction Minnesota Cost And Photos

Liposuction is the elimination of a cosmetic defect that is not the main method of treating obesity. Modern correction technologies allow painless and quickly eliminate fat deposits.

The visual effect after manipulation is more palpable than with weight loss with the help of diets. The purpose of liposuction is not weight loss.

This procedure allows you to find a new contour of the body due to the local elimination of excess fat. If non-compliance with dietary recommendations, the volume of adipose tissue returns within several years.

Liposuction in Minneapolis is not a method for losing weight. If you significantly exceed your personal weight, then before you seek help from a plastic surgeon, you need to lose weight.

You need to follow a diet, proper nutrition and exercise. And only then you can call your surgeon. The safe amount of fat removed is limited and sometimes you can not solve all problems at once.

The most effective procedure for modeling body parts in women:

  • Abdomen,
  • Waist,
  • Thighs,
  • Buttocks,
  • Chin,
  • Cheeks.

In men, greater effectiveness can be seen with liposuction of

  • Breast,
  • Back,
  • Neck-collar zone,
  • Abdomen,
  • Thighs.

The procedure effectively solves the problems:

  • Formation of fatty folds.
  • The formation of saggy and flabby skin, which has lost its elasticity as a result of losing weight.
  • Formation of cellulite in the thigh and buttocks.
  • The appearance of stretch marks.

In which areas are liposuction performed?

The procedure should be carried out in problem areas of the body, which can not be corrected by fitness and diet. Modern medicine offers the possibility of removing excess adipose tissue in the following areas:

  • In the buccal region,
  • In the zone of the lower jaw, chin and neck,
  • In the area under the breast,
  • In the abdomen and lateral areas of the body (waist),
  • In the suprapubic zone,
  • In the subscapular region,
  • In the zone of the back, buttocks, thighs,
  • In the region of the knees and lower leg,
  • In the brachial region,
  • In the area of the forearms.

Indications for operation

The main indication for liposuction is the presence of excess fat in local areas. Modeling of the figure is carried out:

  • With values of body mass index above the average,
  • In the presence of elastic skin,
  • With a good state of health, the absence of acute pathological processes and severe somatic diseases,
  • If there is no significant effect from regular exercise and dieting.

Contraindications

You can not perform plastic surgery under the following conditions:

  • Serious chronic diseases of internal organs.
  • The oncological process.
  • Violation of blood clotting.
  • Acute viral and catarrhal pathologies.
  • Febrile state.
  • Pregnancy and lactation.
  • Diabetes.
  • Varicose veins of the lower limbs and thrombophlebitis.
  • Autoimmune processes.
  • Skin lesion at the site of manipulation by a fungus.
  • Bacterial or viral skin infection.
  • Mental diseases.
  • It is forbidden to perform the procedure for individual intolerance of drugs used for anesthesia.

Anesthesia

The procedure for eliminating body fat is in most cases carried out under local anesthesia. With the introduction of local anesthetic, the risk of side effects is minimized. Liposuction can be performed under general anesthesia.

The choice of this variant of anesthesia depends on the scale of the intervention and the functional state of the patient’s body. The choice of the method of anesthesia in each case is decided individually after a thorough examination of the surgeon and consultation with an anesthesiologist.

Ultrasonic liposuction

The elimination of fat deposits by the method of cavitation (ultrasound) refers to physiotherapeutic procedures. This is a safe modeling of the body, carried out by a special apparatus – lipocavitator.

Mechanism of action:

  • Under the influence of ultrasonic waves, the membrane of the fat cells is destroyed.
  • Significantly decreases the density of fat (it liquefies).
  • With a non-invasive method, emulsified fat is gradually excreted through the lymphatic ducts.
  • To accelerate the effectiveness of the procedure, after a preliminary exposure to the fat tissue by ultrasound, a special cannula is inserted under the skin. Through it with the help of vacuum suction emulsified fat is pumped out.

Advantages of ultrasonic liposuction:

  • Safety of the method.
  • Possibility of non-invasive correction.
  • Proven effectiveness.
  • Long-term effect.

The ultrasound method is recommended to be performed in the absence of weight loss with diet and regular physical exercises. After the procedure, a persistent loss of body weight is noted.

Ultrasound completely destroys fat cells (with normal weight loss there is a decrease in fat). This gives a guarantee that excess weight will not return after a while after the manipulation.

Disadvantages: the possibility of traumatization of internal organs and tissues with insufficient qualification of a doctor or a thin fat layer.
