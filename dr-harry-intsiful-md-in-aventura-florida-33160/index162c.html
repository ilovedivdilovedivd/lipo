Statement: Dr. Harry Intsiful is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Aventura, Florida, with over 15 years of experience. Known as Dr. Slimthick by his patients due to the stunningly curvy silhouettes he helps them achieve, he has become a highly sought-out surgeon in his field. His surgical expertise ranges from face and breast enhancement to body contouring procedures.

At his private practice, Dolls Plastic Surgery, Miami-area patients seek out Dr. Intsiful for his skills in Brazilian butt lift, breast augmentation, mastopexy, liposuction and liposculpture, ab sketching, thigh lift, arm lift, and tummy tucks.

He performs a wide range of facial aesthetic surgeries, including facelift, rhinoplasty, necklift, eyelid surgery, and otoplasty. Dr. Intsiful also offers nonsurgical and minimally invasive aesthetic treatments like Botox, J-Plasma, dermal fillers, scar revision, and vaginal rejuvenation such as ThermiVa.

Dr. Intsiful also has a strong and passionate interest in reconstructive surgery. He received specialized training in craniofacial and hand procedures and the surgical treatment of burns.

Dr. Intsiful earned his medical degree from Howard University. He then underwent his general surgery training at Brooklyn Hospital Center, which was followed by his plastic surgery residency at Georgia Health Sciences University in Augusta, Georgia. There, he honed his aesthetic surgical skills and knowledge, training under some of the most respected surgeons in the field.

After finishing his training at GHSU, Dr. Intsiful went on to complete two surgical fellowships. The first was in hand surgery at Stony Brook University Hospital, while the other was in burn surgery at Jacobi Medical Center in New York City.

Dr. Intsiful is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
