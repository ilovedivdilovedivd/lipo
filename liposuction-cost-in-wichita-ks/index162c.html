Liposuction in Wichita is the most commonly performed plastic surgery in the world. The development of this method goes in two main directions.

On the one hand, surgeons develop solutions that are introduced into the layer of subcutaneous fat before the operation.

These solutions loosen fat, making it easier to remove, and reduce blood loss during surgery, which is why it is now possible to remove more fat than it did ten years ago, and on the other hand, the methods of destroying the fat cells themselves are being improved. Cells can be destroyed mechanically, as well as using ultrasound, an electromechanical field and even a laser.

Why do people rely on liposuction instead of losing the weight?

Indications for this operation are the fat deposits that change the contours of the body, for example, the so-called “ears on hips”, excessive sweating of the armpits, in this case ultrasound liposuction, gynecomastia (this is a pathological condition where men are enlarged by breast enlargement due to subcutaneous fat).

Liposuction can also be performed as an addition to other operations that improve the contours of the face and body.

All operations have contraindications and liposuction is not an exception. Liposuction is contraindicated in people with severe concomitant pathology.

For example, ischemic heart disease, arterial hypertension, heart disease, cancer, varicose veins of the lower extremities. Liposuction is not a weight loss method and is not a substitute for diet and exercise. Liposuction in Wichita – sometimes called fat aspiration is the removal of fat by suction. For example, this is the most common operation in the US, performed even more often than removing the hernia.

The technique of the operation was popularized by the French gynecologist, who suggested the idea of using cannulas (tubes) used for scraping the uterus, for fat extraction. In 1921, he tried to remove some fat from the knees of the famous ballerina, but the result was tragic – she had to eventually amputate the leg!

Liposuction FAT

Today, the best results are obtained when the operation is made for young and healthy women.

However, a satisfactory effect is observed in middle-aged women who are not too deviated from ideal weight and have a good skin tone. The skin should be elastic enough to normally contract after removing fat.

But the teenagers will be disappointed: liposuction does not guarantee the transformation of any girl into Kate Moss. Such an operation will not make fat legs thin – this is not a method of treating obesity.

It is not recommended to do liposuction to young girls, as their weight stabilizes for several years. If the girl underwent liposuction, and then she gained weight, she could get a disproportionate figure, because the new fat cells can grow unevenly.

However, liposuction is extremely effective for removing fat in diet-resistant areas of the body, such as, for example, the outer or inner sides of the thighs.
