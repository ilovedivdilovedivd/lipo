How Long After Smart Lipo Can One Exercise?
2-3 weeks
Aug 4, 2021
Most doctors recommend that patients start or restart an exercise regimen about 2-3 weeks after liposuction to best enhance results, although (light) general activity can be resumed 1-2 days after. We do recommend that our patients MOVE after surgery to speed up their recovery.
SmartLipo for extra skin on chin?
No
Aug 4, 2021
If your concern is extra/sagging skin, liposuction will not be a good resolution to this problem as liposuction is used to remove unwanted fat and can leave to access the skin. For tightening the skin a mini lift or tuck would be recommended, you could combine this with liposuction as well. Double chin surgery, such as a chin tuck, neck lift or chin liposuction, may help you look younger or thinner. The procedures can shape the area, remove excess fat and smooth the skin.
If I get Smart Lipo on my upper and lower abdomen, do I also have to do my flanks? Or can I opt-out of
You can opt-out
Aug 4, 2021
Some individuals may not have enough fat to remove from the flanks, but if you do and you decide to forgo this area while getting your abdomen treated you may regret leaving that area out of your treatment plan. Consult with your surgeon on what they would recommend based on how much fat you carry in that area.
2 c-sections? Am I a good candidate for Smart Lipo on the abdomen?
Yes
Aug 4, 2021
It is ideal for patients who have specific areas of localized fat deposits and have tried unsuccessfully to eliminate them through diet, exercise and weight loss.
Does outer thigh/ “saddle bag” lipo produce good results, long term?
Yes
Aug 4, 2021
Lipo targeted on the thighs is more effective for individuals with enough elasticity in their thighs to contour well after the procedure. This will help eliminate a crepey or loose appearance.
What’s thhe best way to sleep after Smart Lipo and should my diet include high fatty acids post op?
Don’t sleep lying flat
Aug 4, 2021
Extending your body straight from head to toe may put pressure on your incisions and hamper their healing. It’s best to find a comfortable position that relieves this pressure and allows those sutures to stay in place without any pulling or stretching. Although sleeping on your side may be acceptable, many may find themselves tossing and turning, which can cause wounds to be pulled or open. Because you can’t sleep on your tummy, we recommend finding the most comfortable position on your back.
Am I a candidate for Smart Lipo on my back?
Yes
Aug 4, 2021
It is ideal for patients who have specific areas of localized fat deposits and have tried unsuccessfully to eliminate them through diet, exercise and weight loss. The addition of laser heat with the SmartLipo procedure provides an added benefit of tightening the skin. Liposuction alone does not provide any skin tightening benefits, so there is an increased possibility of dimpling or sagging after the procedure, particularly with older patients who have lost more of the elasticity of the skin.
