Tallahassee Liposuction Cost

In addition to weight loss methods with the use of diets and sports, there is a fast, but a surgical way of pumping out fatty deposits is liposuction.

The mechanism of the procedure is to destroy the fat layer and remove the formed “liquid” from the body.

Liposuction in Tallahassee is considered a plastic surgery, necessary for correction of the body – it is performed with the purpose of removing fats in problem areas.

Surgeons perform liposuction on different parts of the body, both to women and to men. Liposuction of the abdomen, thighs, hands, chin, knees and buttocks is common.

What liposuction methods are used, and how this will be done will be discussed in this article.

The process of fat removal with liposuction is a surgical operation performed under general anesthesia with a long period of recovery.

All about draining fat surgically

The operation of liposuction of the body has different techniques: surgical removal of fat and non-surgical exposure. In turn, they are divided into several types.

Classical Liposuction

This type of pumping out fats requires general anesthesia, compliance with indications and contraindications.

The operation can take place in one of three ways:

Dry. This is the very first method of liposuction. Incisions of 3-5 mm are made on the skin and through them a special instrument is inserted into the adipose tissue – the cannula, which is connected to the vacuum pump. Input and output in the adipose tissue produces fan-shaped – it promotes the breakdown of fat and pulling out the mass outward with device. The procedure is traumatic, during its conduct a lot of blood is lost and big hematomas on the body remain. After 2-3 months the incisions will heal, there will be small bands or “circles” on the skin.

Wet. This liposuction is called in another way – luminescent. Performed in the same way as in the dry process, only in the incision is introduced a local anesthetic drug, which dilutes fatty tissue. The presented type of liposuction is less traumatic, therefore it is more popular.

Tumescent. The most common form of liposuction is the classical method. The operation is performed traditionally – only before making cuts on the skin, it is done by special means. This solution helps to thin the fat for several minutes, which facilitates the surgeon pumping out the fat. But the duration of recovery remains the same – not less than 3 months.

Laser liposuction in Tallahassee

A laser form of liposuction was invented in Italy. The operation consists in the fact that a complex procedure of action takes place – the cleavage of adipose tissue and the coagulation (union) of the vessels. The laser radiation is fed through a micro-cannula – its size is no more than 1 mm. Mechanical and thermal effects destroy fat cells and cauterize vessels.

The advantages of this type of liposuction are:

  • minimum formation of hematomas;
  • low traumatic danger;
  • Collagen production, necessary for tightening the skin.

The operation is performed under local anesthesia. Restoration lasts 3-4 days, but you need to wear compression underwear for a long time.

Water jet type

This is the newest operating technique for liposuction. Its mechanism consists in the destruction of adipose tissue by a jet of aqueous solution, as a result of which the fat decomposes and quietly departs from other tissues. The resulting mixture of water and fat is simply pumped out. The solution produces anesthesia and simultaneous narrowing of the vessels. It is served with a special device, which creates the necessary pressure to draw the liquid. Restoration lasts 3-4 days, the operation is painless and does not leave a hematoma.

Radio frequency

It differs in that radiofrequency energy is used for the breakdown of fats. For the operation, two electrodes are applied at once, the inner electrode is placed in adipose tissue and serves as a suction tube and a temperature sensor.

Vibration

The peculiarity of this liposuction – placed in the cannula fat – produces short rotational movements. During a single operation, a large amount of fat is removed, and the surgeon does not require enormous efforts for manipulation. This kind is used to remove fat from delicate areas – the face, chin, neck.

Ultrasound

The surgeon implements an incision in the designated areas and introduces an ultrasound probe. Feeding ultrasound helps to destroy fat cells forever, so the result is long-term and persistent. The operation is painless and highly effective. For one procedure you can remove up to 17 lb of fat. The skin after it becomes supple and smooth.

On the prohibitions and consequences

Decide on a similar removal of fat should only after the lack of results from other ways to lose weight. This is due to the presence of contraindications, where the following are distinguished:

  1. children under 18 years of age;
  2. heart and vascular disease;
  3. diabetes;
  4. oncological formations;
  5. pregnancy;
  6. chronic diseases.

Before deciding on an operation, it is necessary to compare the risk from the operation and the result obtained.

Even with liposuction to a healthy person, various complications can arise:

  1. subcutaneous hematomas;
  2. severe pain;
  3. suppurative inflammation of skin incision sites;
  4. formation of gross scars;
  5. fat embolism.

Do not insist on liposuction during an exacerbation of chronic diseases, menstruation and gastrointestinal problems.
