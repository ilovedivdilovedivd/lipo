Doctors

Pradnya Apte, BDS

Name: Pradnya Apte, BDS
Statement: Dr. Pradnya Apte is a specialist registered dentist with nearly 30 years in practice. Based in Exeter in Devon, England, she received her bachelor of dental surgery (BDS) degree in Liverpool and is board-certified by the General Dental Service (GDS) board.

In 2005, Dr. Apte pivoted from dentistry to medical aesthetics. “Aesthetic medicine has changed so much over the last 16 years I have been practicing,” she says. “With so many changes and improvements in products and results that we can now achieve, I felt that this was the career for me.”

Dr. Apte offers a range of medical aesthetic treatments at her Exeter medspa, Skin Southwest. Popular options include lip augmentation and nonsurgical rhinoplasty via dermal fillers like Ellansé and Juvéderm, as well as neuromodulator injections such as Botox, to smooth the appearance of wrinkles.

Dr. Apte is also skilled in a broad range of noninvasive and minimally invasive treatments that can improve the appearance of both face and body. Her treatment menu includes chemical peels, advanced electrolysis hair removal, Endymed, intense pulsed light (IPL or photofacial), Trusculpt Flex, and more. “I am very passionate about such treatments and aim to achieve a natural, refreshed, and rejuvenated appearance for my clients, utilising these minimally invasive treatments and techniques,” she says.

With every patient she sees, Dr. Apte’s mission is to connect with them on a personal level and align on the best treatment plan to achieve their aesthetic goals and inspire confidence.

“I love nothing more than being an artist as I work on my patients. Aesthetic medicine has to be an art form for enhancing the patients’ own beauty with subtle yet real changes,” she says. “I create natural-looking results with all my treatments. My honesty, my reviews, and the results I deliver speak for themselves.”

Whether your concerns include ageing skin, acne scars, hyperpigmentation, pronounced veins, stubborn fat, or other issues, Dr. Apte can help address them, leaving you feeling more beautiful and confident.
