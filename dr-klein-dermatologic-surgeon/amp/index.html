I'm 9 months post tummy tuck and 19 days post op abdominal liposuction. Did I mess up my results?
Normal Post Liposuction Inflammation
Apr 10, 2019
After abdominal liposuction, many patients experience a period of “lumpiness”. During the initial 6-8 weeks, you may “feel” firm inflammatory nodules, and by the end of the day, you can actually see them. This usually resolves within the first couple months, but can take 4-6 months for some patients. As long as the abdomen feels firm, you are experiencing postoperative inflammation and cannot truely evaluate your results. It will improve.
