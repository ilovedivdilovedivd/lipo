His innovative contributions encompass pioneering techniques such as radiofrequency labiaplasty for the minora, introducing procedures like the “Barbie” and Hybrid Look labiaplasty, and inventing transformative approaches like the Medial Curvilinear Labia Majoraplasty, central and lateral clitoral hood reductions.
