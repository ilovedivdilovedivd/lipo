Mexico Liposuction Prices And Before After Photos

Liposuction in Mexico is an operational removal of local surpluses of fatty tissue.

Liposuction helps to emphasize the contours of the face, adjust the contours of the body, form a harmonious silhouette, but is not a method of radical weight loss, in one procedure you can safely remove no more than 4% of the total mass of adipose tissue.

Depending on the volume of the planned procedure and the part of the body that needs correction, you will be offered the most effective way of performing the surgery: laser liposuction, RF (radiofrequency) liposuction or classical aspiration liposuction. A combination of methods is possible.

Age and indications

With the inability to cope with fat deposits are faced by patients of all ages, most of them do not have excess weight, many regularly go in for sports, but, nevertheless, can not get rid of, for example, the “second chin” or “riding breeches” on the hips.

If the fatty deposits are not large, then the ideal method will be laser liposuction. Its ptos consists of low traumatism and the absence of cuts on the skin. The fat tissue is melted by the action of the laser through a small puncture. If the fat deposits are more significant, then it is more rational to offer classical or Rf-liposuction.

For areas with reduced skin tone, we recommend that RF liposuction, as the most effective and modern method. Its huge advantage lies in the possibility of affecting the skin for its best reduction and tonus after the procedure.

Contraindications.

How to prepare for the surgery?

Liposuction in Mexico can be contraindicated in varicose veins in the areas of operation, as well as patients older than 60 years old.

Chin Liposuction Mexico

Duration of the operation

The duration of the operation, depending on the volume, is from 30 minutes to 2-3 hours.

Anesthesia

With liposuction, both local anesthesia and general anesthesia can be used. The choice of the preferred type of anesthesia depends on the planned scope of the operation.

Hospital stay

Stay in the hospital is 1-2 days, depending on the complexity of the liposuction performed.

Recovery after the procedure

Within a month after the operation, it is necessary to wear special compression clothes, during this period it is not possible to visit the sauna, sauna and solarium. After about 1-2 weeks, you can do the usual things and go to work. Sport activities can be resumed within a month after surgery.

Evaluation of result

The first results you will see a month after liposuction, the final effect will manifest after 3-4 months. Such procedures as massage, wraps, pressotherapy will help you bring the result of the operation to perfection.

Effect duration

The effect of liposuction is constant while maintaining a stable weight or slight fluctuations in it.
