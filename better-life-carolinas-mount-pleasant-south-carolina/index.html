I’ve been on Xyzal for my hives problem, Is it safe to have filler injections then?
Filler and Xyzal
Feb 25, 2019
Taking Xyzal for hives is not a contraindication to using a cosmetic filler injection assuming you are not allergic to anything in the product being used. You can always obtain a list of ingredients from the manufacturer or provider if needed. Good luck!

Is it ok to have Botox injected around my eyes with a minor eye infection?
eye infection and botox
Sep 4, 2018
Hello. I know you are probably anxious to get your botox done. However, it is best to wait until the eye infection clears up. Putting a needle in the skin around the eyes could make matters worse. Possibly spreading the infection or prolonging the healing process. It is not worth the risk. Good luck with your eye.
How Many Botox Units for 11's or Frown Lines?
Christine Coe CRNA
Aug 20, 2018
20 units is a great dose to start with. I think you need more time to evaluate. Remember botox takes 3-14 days for the full effect. If you are unhappy give your injector a chance and go back in after 2 weeks. You may need a touch up. I’m sure your injector wants you to be happy and be a returning customer. I chart how much I am giving in specific locations, then we can evaluate and give more or less if needed the next time. Good luck.
