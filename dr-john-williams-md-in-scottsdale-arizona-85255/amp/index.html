Can permanent vertical muscle suture from Tummy Tuck be removed?
Unsuccessful muscle repair (plication)
Sep 16, 2009
Most likely the sutures used to bring together your separated rectus muscles have broken. This can happen if there is a sudden increase in intra-abdominal pressure after surgery (like a sneeze or vigorous cough) or the sutures were placed under too much tension, or both.
It is doubtful that you are rejecting the suture.
