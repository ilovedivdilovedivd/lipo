Doctors

Randy Sanovich, DDS

Name: Randy Sanovich, DDS
Statement: As a dual board-certified surgeon in oral, maxillofacial, and facial cosmetic surgery, Dr. Randy Sanovich is committed to providing optimum results for his patients.

Dr. Sanovich’s extensive training and experience make him a recognized leader in the latest advancements of oral and facial reconstructive procedures. His approach to facial rejuvenation promotes natural surgical results without looking artificial. In addition to facial cosmetic surgery, Dr. Sanovich specializes in jaw reconstructive surgery, including jaw repositioning surgery, TMJ surgery, bone grafting and dental implant placement.

Dr. Sanovich received his Bachelor of Science degree in marketing from the University of Utah, and his dental degree from Virginia Commonwealth University. He then completed his training in oral and maxillofacial surgery from the University of Florida. His special interest in facial cosmetic surgery led him to complete a highly sought-after fellowship in facial cosmetic and reconstructive surgery with Dr. John Griffin in Columbus, Mississippi.

“I am dedicated to providing the best of art and science in facial plastic surgery as well as oral & maxillofacial surgery to accommodate all of my patient’s needs with as few appointments as possible,” says Dr. Sanovich.

Dr. Sanovich’s reputation for his outstanding patient care stems from the way he listens to and understands his patients’ needs and concerns. He utilizes cutting-edge medical technology, such as 3D imagery and virtual surgical planning, to help his patients visualize their results. Dr. Sanovich then crafts an individualized treatment plan to achieve the patient’s goals. From the first phone call to the last follow-up visit, the Dallas Surgical Arts team strives to create a personal relationship and offer the best of care to every patient.

Dr. Sanovich loves to share his expertise through teaching oral and maxillofacial surgery residents in Dallas. He is also the director of the esteemed American Academy of Cosmetic Surgery fellowship program at Dallas Surgical Arts, where he is responsible for training surgeons in advanced cosmetic surgery and leadership skills.

Dr. Sanovich and his wife Amber live in Dallas with their three young children. Outside of his busy practice, he enjoys spending time with his family, teaching his children how to wake-surf at Lake Lewisville. He also enjoys playing guitar and listening to music.
