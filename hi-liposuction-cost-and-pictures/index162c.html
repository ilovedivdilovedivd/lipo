To date, this operation is almost the only effective method of body correction.

Just one procedure for a long time is able to save the patient from local fat deposits in the so-called problem areas (thighs, buttocks, abdomen), returning the slender shape of the body and simulating the volumes.

Types of liposuction in Honolulu

Liposuction is carried out by the main methods developed to date and positively proven in the whole world:

  • Mechanical;
  • Ultrasound;
  • Laser.

The appointment and conduct of a particular method of lipolysis depends, as a rule, on the physician. Based on the survey, the patient’s level of health and the age of the problem, the doctor can suggest several solutions to the problem.

For example, laser liposuction is indicated to patients with excess fat in “delicate” areas such as cheeks, hands, chin and neck, and ultrasonic lipolysis is effective when it is necessary to “pump out” a small amount of adipose tissue from the problem area.

This type of plastic surgery is the most famous in the field of aesthetic surgery. It is based on surgical intervention, through which fat is removed from the problem areas using special equipment and tubes.

This kind of lipolysis is carried out with the greatest caution, because such an intervention and unexpected pumping out of the subcutaneous fat can lead to injuries of various kinds.

In this regard, this procedure is ranked by surgeons as complex. The operation is performed under anesthesia. It has a relatively long recovery period.

Before mechanical liposuction, the patient must undergo a series of measures aimed at preparing for the operation. In particular, two weeks before the operation, the patient is forbidden to take alcohol or smoke. During this period it is necessary to have all the relevant tests (the doctor at the consultation specifies which ones).

Possible complications:

  • Numbness of the tissues;
  • High probability of occurrence of scars;
  • Vascular damage;
  • swelling;
  • Increased pain sensitivity.

Laser liposuction

This method of lipolysis, as well as ultrasound liposuction, refers to low-traumatic methods of body correction, which allows not only to solve the problem of excessive fat deposits, but also to accelerate the processes of tissue recovery.

Specialized laser equipment, oriented to safe liposuction, promotes effective destruction of fat cell conglomerations both in the so-called problem areas and in those parts of the body where other methods of lipolysis are either not used or are used with great care.

During laser liposuction, thermal radiation, acting on the surface of the skin, penetrates deep into the area of subcutaneous fat, provoking a sharp temperature change in the cells. Thus, there is destruction of fat cell membranes; The fat tissue itself becomes viscous enough to be easily removed by lipolysis, or by means of stimulating natural elimination procedures.

However, the main advantage of laser liposuction in Honolulu is not so much effective removal of surplus fatty tissue, as stimulation of the skin to rapid regeneration. Thermal action on the connective tissue activates the production of the basic protein complexes responsible for the elasticity of the skin.

A natural increase in the functional characteristics of the epidermis significantly shortens the rehabilitation period and makes liposuction more safe. In addition, during laser liposuction, thermal radiation effectively coagulates the vessels, which avoids the occurrence of hematomas after the procedure.

Thus, laser liposuction has the following advantages over other methods of lipolysis:

  1. Painlessness (local anesthesia);
  2. Absence of bruises and blood loss;
  3. The minimum recovery period;
  4. Possibility of processing delicate zones;
  5. Absence of postoperative scars;
  6. Possibility of holding in any season.

After laser liposuction does not require hospitalization. Almost immediately after surgery, the patient can go home. For comparison, ultrasound liposuction means a patient’s stay in the hospital from several hours to a day.

When can liposuction be performed?

With excess fatty deposits of one desire to lose weight, of course, not enough. You need to strive in every way, set goals and try to achieve them. Most of our patients decided on liposuction, because they did not get a satisfactory result from other methods of correction of the body.

Moreover, it should be noted that these methods are by no means ineffective, they are ineffective in the conditions of one particular person.

However, it is possible to do liposuction for every person, and it’s not even the cost of surgery or the patient’s indecision. Liposuction has always implied a serious surgical intervention, and, consequently, the patient’s body must have exceptional health in order to postpone the recovery period without complications.

Thus, with any method of pumping out subcutaneous fat, the patient should be prepared as much as possible for the procedure. For this purpose, a month before the operation, the following is excluded:

  • Drinking alcohol;
  • Smoking;
  • Antibiotics or substances weaken the immune system;
  • Intense physical activity.
