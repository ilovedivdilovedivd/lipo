Doctors

Timothy G. Rodgers, MD, FAAD

Name: Timothy G. Rodgers, MD, FAAD
Statement: Dr. Timothy G. Rodgers is a board-certified dermatologist celebrated for his exceptional proficiency in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide spectrum of skin conditions. He is deeply committed to delivering the highest level of care to his patients, continuously staying abreast of the latest advancements in dermatology to offer the most effective treatments available.

Hailing from Topeka, Kansas, Dr. Rodgers earned his B.S. in Journalism from the University of Kansas. Following his military service, he attained his Doctor of Medicine degree from Northwestern University in Chicago. He then completed a dermatology residency at the prestigious University of Michigan Medical Center’s Department of Dermatology in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Dr. Rodgers holds the distinction of being a Diplomate of the American Board of Dermatology and a Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.

Operating from his practice in Frisco, Texas, Dr. Rodgers provides personalized and holistic care to his patients. He collaborates closely with each individual to craft a customized treatment plan tailored to their specific needs and concerns. Whether patients grapple with acne, eczema, psoriasis, or another skin condition, Dr. Rodgers possesses the knowledge and experience essential to guide them toward achieving healthy, radiant skin.
