Liposuction Prices In Chicago IL

Liposuction in Chicago is a cosmetology or bariatric surgery for surgically changing the nature of fat deposition in a specific area (or several parts of the body), for example, on the abdomen, thighs, buttocks. In this case, liposuction is not a method of treating obesity, since it does not affect the pathogenesis of this disease and does not allow the removal of significant amounts of fat. However, the visual effect of liposuction for a figure can be more significant than when losing the same fat mass in other ways.

Indications

Women often turn to liposuction to remove the so-called “ears on the hips”, as well as fat deposits on the abdomen, waist, hips, buttocks, forearm, back, calves, knees and soon.

Men in the majority want to remove the deposits on the neck, chest, back, waist, abdomen and buttocks.

A suitable candidate for liposuction:

  • Weight average or slightly above average;
  • Elastic, supple skin;
  • Good general health;
  • The presence of local fatty deposits, which do not disappear even with the observance of diet and exercise.

Types of liposuction:

  • Tumescent
  • Ultrasound
  • Laser
  • RF liposuction.

Possible complications

After the operation, numbness of some parts of the body may occur. If the bandage was removed prematurely, the body may have scars or bruises. In the first few weeks, physical activity is not recommended.

As a rule, indications for the operation are fat deposits that arise in the abdomen, inner thighs, buttocks, hands, calves. Liposuction in Chicago is not carried out for people with acute and serious chronic diseases, diseases of internal organs, diabetes, circulatory system diseases.

Infectious complications are extremely dangerous for health, the infection spreads throughout the subcutaneous space involved in the operation, causing severe purulent lesions.

10 days before the liposuction is prohibited to take drugs that affect blood clotting, smoking. It is recommended to have the procedure of bowel cleansing and to have all the necessary tests.

If contraindications are not revealed, the date of the operation is appointed. The duration of liposuction depends on the zone of intervention.

As a rule, it is from 30 minutes to several hours. Taking into account the functional state of the body, local or general anesthesia is performed.
