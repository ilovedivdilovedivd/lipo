Affecting complexes about the imperfection of the body, they know what is liposuction. The ability to adjust the contours of the body by sucking off subcutaneous fat with a special vacuum device has appeared relatively recently.

Today, laser liposuction or any other kind of surgical correction of appearance is available to a much larger number of people. Fat deposits are one of the signs that the body needs help.

They can occur on the abdomen, internal or external surface of the thighs, calf, back, forearms and chin.

Excess fat slows down the motor activity, makes breathing difficult and creates a lot of psychological complexes, which only aggravate the situation.

Fight this problem with the help of an experienced doctor who will find the cause of the disease, make a treatment plan and develop a special diet to combat excess weight and excessive fat.

But there are times when rational nutrition and exercise, mesotherapy for weight loss can not help reduce the size of the waist or smooth the hated “riding breeches” on the hips. In this situation, many decide on a radical step, which is the operation of liposuction.

Indications for the procedure

Mechanical fat removal is not a panacea for excess weight. Excess weight is usually associated with any disorders in the body. Therefore, if there was such a problem, it is necessary first to eliminate its cause, and then to fight with the investigation.

But, if the patient has undergone a course of treatment, adheres to proper nutrition and actively engages in sports, then it is possible that it is the liposuction, that can save you from the psychological complexes that have arisen because of the small shortcomings of the body.

Liposuction can help get rid of local adipose tissue accumulations in such areas of the body where it is difficult to do this with the help of physical exercises:

  • anterior abdominal wall;
  • chin area;
  • external surface of thighs;
  • knees.

This type of plastic surgery is used in women to remove the characteristic tubercle on the neck, resembling a hump and arising during the menopause. A person should be absolutely healthy for liposuction. Before the procedure, it is necessary to undergo a test and have all the necessary tests to avoid the occurrence of serious complications during and after the operation. 3-4 weeks before the procedure, the patient should give up smoking and spicy food. Such measures are necessary, so that the process of tissue regeneration goes faster.

After removal of fatty tissue, you should strictly follow the doctor’s recommendations, follow the diet and refrain for a month from physical activity. But after a forced interruption, which is necessary for the body in the postoperative period, it is necessary to resume training. Otherwise, the remaining fat tissue will again grow, and all measures for the return of harmony are futile.

Contraindications to surgical removal of fat

Absolute contraindications to the use of this method of correction of the body, regardless of its type, are recognized:

  • abnormal metabolism, including obesity and diabetes mellitus;
  • pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding;
  • impaired renal and hepatic function;
  • malignant tumors;
  • low coagulation rate;
  • infectious diseases;
  • hypertension;
  • thrombophlebitis;
  • anemia;
  • peptic ulcer of stomach and duodenum;
  • age to 18 years.

When there is at least one such factor in the patient’s history, liposuction is strictly prohibited. If the doctor suggests the procedure, despite the presence of contraindications, then he is not a professional plastic surgeon and poses the possibility of obtaining a decent profit above the patient’s health, which is unacceptable.

If you really want to radically change the body shape and get rid of extra centimeters in any particular area, but there are contraindications, or simply no money, do not despair. In most cases, when small fat deposits are acquired, rather than hereditary, it is always possible to solve the problem without surgical intervention. The question, whether there is an alternative to liposuction, always remains relevant.
