What is liposuction and what types of liposuction are available?

Liposuction in Santo Domingo is a surgical method of correcting the shape of the body by reducing fat tissue in its problem areas.

This procedure is not a panacea, so when combating obesity, cellulite and metabolic disorders, you should pay attention to other methods that will have a more effective effect.

People with obesity must first reduce the body mass index to an acceptable rate under the supervision of their dietician and only then, if there is such a need, will turn to the procedure of liposuction.

Liposuction is more intended for people who, due to genetic predisposition, tend to accumulate fat in certain places, which can not be removed with diet and active physical exercises.

At the moment, there are many new modern hardware techniques that are aimed at reducing body weight and gaining its beautiful contours. But, unfortunately, they are not always effective when it comes to fat deposits that are associated with hereditary features of the figure.

Therefore, such people can recommend liposuction as a way of acquiring an ideal physical form. This method, as a rule, is used to correct such zones as: the neck under the chin, the shoulders, the back, abdomen, waist, chest, upper outer and inner thighs, the inside of the knees and so on.

Liposuction in Santo Domingo has no age restrictions, it can be carried out at any age. In order to perform this procedure, you must be physically and emotionally in good shape, so that there are no health problems.

It is necessary to undergo a complete examination, to pass all tests in order to exclude the risk of complications. Before it is performed, you should also carefully consult a plastic surgeon who will perform the operation.

Ask about all the details of liposuction, how the procedure will be performed, what results you can get after it, how long recovery period will last.

Liposuction is usually performed under general anesthesia, if the area of work is significant, in the case of working with small areas, local anesthesia is used.

The duration of the operation depends on which area of the body liposuction will be used, what amount of fat will need to be pumped out and the amount of work involved. The quality of the result will depend on the professionalism of the surgeon, so choose a good specialist who has established himself as a true professional in his field.

In order to carry out this procedure, a special vacuum device is used with attached to it special tubes (cannulas) that are placed in the human body through small incisions in the skin and through them the suction of excess fat is produced.

All that is pumped out, then you can use to give volume to other parts of the body, which lacks it, it can be lips and chest and pop. This procedure is called lipofilling. After liposuction, patients, depending on their condition, can stay in the clinic for several hours to several days.

Your surgeon must choose in advance for you special compression underwear, which will need to be worn after the procedure for six to eight weeks, as it helps, quickly gets rid of swelling and make the skin more tight and elastic at the site of exposure.

It is worth remembering, that the results can not appear instantaneous, visible changes can be obtained only after three to four weeks after the operation, and the final result can be obtained only after two months.

The biggest bruises, edema, bruises will be within 2-3 weeks, all other manifestations of the operation on the skin will take place for about a whole month. In the first days after liposuction, it is normal to feel slight pain and numbness in the areas where it was performed, and some people may feel small seals that eventually will dissolve on their own.

Wa can take a shower and wash in a few days, but lying in a hot bath, going to the sauna is not recommended for two months.

Various SPA procedures in the place where liposuction was performed, it is better to start not earlier than a month. Physical exercises and sports can begin only after a month, since you have liposuction.

To accelerate the recovery process, it is necessary to undergo a course of drainage-lymphatic massage for three weeks. Eat more fruits, greens and healthy food so that the body is filled with vitamins and nutrients. It is not necessary to drink alcoholic beverages on the first day after liposuction and there is too much sweetness within the next year.

It should be remembered that small postoperative scars that will disappear over time should be protected from direct sunlight during the first six months. Therefore, sunbathing under the scorching sun and visiting the solarium during this time period is not recommended.

It is not necessary in the first month, to use a cream with various active substances. The result after the procedure is sufficiently persistent and long-term, provided that the person continues to maintain his physical form in good condition, regularly does physical exercises, eats properly and so on.
