Here, experienced surgeons perform liposuction. This method of correcting body contours is to remove excess fat deposits from certain areas.

The operation is technically uncomplicated, has a short recovery period and, subject to the advice of a doctor, guarantees the preservation of the result for a long time.

The price of liposuction in our clinic depends on the number, area and location of the target zones. In one session, you can remove from 0.79-1.58 gallon of fat. Most often, patients turn to correct the following areas of the body:

  • abdomen, sides, waist;
  • hips (external and internal parts);
  • knees;
  • backs, shoulders;
  • face (second chin).

Liposuction can be done in isolation or in combination with abdominoplasty, lifting the hips and buttocks, face lift.

Before the procedure it is necessary to get a surgeon’s consultation and to undergo a series of examinations. The patient is referred for blood tests (general, biochemistry, infection, coagulability), urine, as well as cardiogram, consultation of the therapist and anesthesiologist.

How is surgical liposuction performed?

The method of anesthesia is determined based on the scope and extent of the intervention. The time of the operation depends on the area of the treated area. The procedure can take from 30 minutes. up to 2 hours.

Liposuction begins with the creation of small incisions or punctures in several places in the target zone, through which a cannula is inserted into the subcutaneous fat layer. After the destruction, the fat layer is sucked off with blood and other biological fluids using an aspirator. Cosmetic sutures are applied to the places of incisions.

After liposuction, the recovery period takes several weeks. During the month, it is necessary to wear compression underwear.

During the first weeks after liposuction, you need to limit physical activity, exclude visits to the gym, swimming pool, sauna. However, moderate activity should be maintained, for example, walking 2-3 days after the operation. The first days after liposuction you need to keep a sparing diet, drink enough fluids. Coffee, tea and alcohol use is undesirable.

Places sutures must be protected from direct sunlight. This will help avoid hyperpigmentation. Liposuction is performed using modern methods. Its price can be clarified at a doctor’s appointment.

Surgeons have extensive experience in conducting similar procedures and provide the expected result of operations. The clinic is equipped with modern equipment that makes liposuction safe and effective.

FAQ

What is included in the list of contraindications for liposuction?

  1. Age is less than 18 and over 65 years.
  2. Pregnancy, breastfeeding.
  3. Diabetes.
  4. Obesity.
  5. Exacerbation of systemic pathologies.
  6. Feverish states.
  7. Blood clotting disorders.
  8. Hypertension.
  9. Varicose veins and thrombophlebitis.
  10. Diseases of the cardiovascular system.
  11. Allergy to anesthetics.
  12. Inflammation and skin infection in the target area.
  13. Epilepsy.
  14. Oncopathology.

What complications can occur after liposuction?

After the operation, extensive bruises, soft tissue injuries, and knolls under the skin can remain due to uneven pumping of fat. All these risks can be avoided if the procedure is performed by an experienced surgeon.

Can fat return after liposuction?

During the procedure, the fat cells in the target zone are removed. If you do not control food and do not exercise, then the body will again begin to replenish fat stores.

But now in the areas that have undergone liposuction, the fat layer will be thinner, while the zones that are not amenable to surgery will begin to accumulate fat in a larger volume.

For example, after removing fat from the abdomen, its cluster will concentrate on the hips and buttocks.
