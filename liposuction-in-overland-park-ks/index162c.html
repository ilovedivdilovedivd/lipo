Liposuction In Overland Park KS

Improper diet and a sedentary lifestyle gave rise to a problem in the modern world such as excess fat in the abdomen.

And if the men are quite friendly and live with such a belly, then for every woman this is a tragedy. The tendency to increased fat in the abdomen is inherent in nature.

But it’s good if these deposits do not exceed the norms and look appetizing.

Today, the problem is much more acute – fast food and the lack of post-natal proper care of oneself actually disfigure the body of a woman. If no diets help, you can resort to liposuction.

What is the abdominal liposuction?

Liposuction in Overland Park is a popular surgical procedure for getting rid of fat in the abdomen. This is a fairly painless operation, during which up to 5 liters of fat deposits are removed. As a rule, with small amounts of work, liposuction is performed under local anesthesia.

General anesthesia is done only if the patient has a lot of fat. First, the surgeon breaks the fat deposits by frequent translational movements, after which the vacuum pump pumps the fat out.

Laser liposuction of abdomen

Laser liposuction (laser lipolysis) is a more gentle method compared to the classical version. The difference is that the doctor breaks the fat tissue with a laser beam through the thinnest hole in the abdomen.

Plus, the surgeon does not act blindly – he clearly controls the movement of the laser under the skin, due to the fact that the ray shines through.

Laser liposuction, in contrast to the classical, can remove up to one and a half liters of fat at a time. The main advantage of the laser method is the fact that the skin of the abdomen is restored much faster and better tightened. After the procedure

Depending on how much fat tissue you have removed, you will be released home in a couple of hours or the next day.

After liposuction, it is possible:

  • temperature increasing
  • slight pain in the abdomen
  • partial numbness of the abdomen

All these symptoms are a normal process of recovery period. The mandatory conditions for successful rehabilitation include the wearing of compression underwear for a minimum of 3 weeks.

This will help to avoid stretching the skin and the appearance of bruises and swelling. To a normal way of life and physical activity it is possible to return a month and a half later.

Realize the result of liposuction is possible only after one and a half to two months. If you follow the basic recommendations and try to eat right, a beautiful belly will delight you for a very long time, since liposuction gives a fairly persistent effect.
