Bangkok Liposuction Prices

Cosmetologists have invented a sensational way of fighting with subcutaneous fat: they suck it with a pump!

You look at yourself in the mirror and you think: no, you can not go on like this! Especially annoying belly. We must urgently and seriously take up our body! How? It’s clear how: diet and exercise, exercise and diet! And then you go to the gym and start working on yourself.

Endless workouts in the hall and you feel like a squirrel in the wheel. You can not treat yourself to baking and French fries. And now a year passes. The results, of course, are: fat has become much less, and muscle – much more. Biceps, triceps and quadriceps in normal.

But fat on your stomach … as it was, so it is! What’s the matter? Maybe it’s genetics? Or mistakes in the workout? Or sandwiches, which you ate all school years, washing down with a coca-cola? But why wonder now! The question is: what to do?

There are two ways to solve.

The first is to double physical activity and to tighten diets. And the second is to go to the surgeon for liposuction. It’s very simple: the surgeon makes a hole in the abdomen, inserts the hose, and pumps out excess fat.

Incidentally, a very fashionable operation now! Only in the States each year this procedure is 300 thousand people. But what about guarantees? And is it not dangerous? Let’s find out more in detail.

The last word in the field of “pumping” fat is ultrasonic lipoplasty. Before removing the fat cells, they are destroyed (“melted”) by ultrasonic vibrations.

Ultrasound does not affect the nerves, blood vessels and surrounding tissues, so that there are practically no tumors and bruises after the procedure.

This technique is great for removing the so-called “fibrous” fat – surrounded by muscle tissue. Just this fat usually “upsets” bodybuilders, accumulating in the upper abdomen, above the pelvic bones or on the chest around the nipples. In all cases, ultrasonic liposuction removes fat, leaving almost invisible scars.

Fat cells can be “removed” from under the chin, from the neck, triceps, shoulders, back, calves, from the inner, outer and front thighs and even from the ankles – in short, almost from any part of the body.

Typically, the procedure takes 1-2 hours and is performed on an outpatient basis. Two weeks after surgery, you must wear tight bandages or special compression clothing – to minimize side effects. “Careful” workout can start as early as the first week, and at the pre-operational level the athlete comes out in about six weeks. Traces of the surgeon’s work are usually visible for 18-20 days, and “a completely new person” you become after six months.

At first glance, everything looks easy and simple. However, even the simplest operation still remains a surgical intervention. Liposuction is not an expensive pleasure ($2000-$4000), it requires anesthesia and in any case the body traumatizes.

The chances of infection are negligible. But patients after surgery often experience pain and discomfort, and the skin does not always restore its former elasticity. As for the “terrible stories”, about which they write in the newspapers, they, alas, are not far from the truth.

Sometimes, that “innocent” cosmetic procedure was ended tragically irreversible deformities or even death of the patient. Of course, such cases are extremely rare, but who wants to get into “one percent failure”?

Therefore it is very important to find a good surgeon. And, of course, you need to frankly tell him about yourself all: what problems were and is with health, what medicines you took. Now, when we know what is liposuction, it remains to answer one question: Do you need this personally?

If you participate in competitions it definitely is not! Of course, the procedure looks tempting. After all, on a precompetitive strict diet you lose not only fat, but also some part of the “mass”. And liposuction removes only fat, without any damage to the muscles. But not everything is so smooth.

Even if the doctor promises a quick recovery, it can take up to three months.

And one more problem is the elasticity of the skin. Since together with the fat cells “goes” part of the connective tissue, the skin can become flabby. We’ll have to find a way out: pull up the skin, get involved in new operations. Liposuction can fully satisfy the average person, but it never goes to the level of 4% fat. For a bodybuilder, a very precise calculation is necessary here.

So, is it worth the risk? You need to face the truth: perhaps you will get rid of the stomach forever – but the fat will find new places for deposits! No matter how advanced science, but sport and a reasonable diet will always remain the best means to build a slender, beautiful body. Sometimes as a result of surgical “operations” patients received legs of different lengths, bags of fat in the most unexpected places. They had to come back to the operating room again and again.

Some become a kind of “drug addicts of plastic surgery.” It is peculiar for a person to get involved in new pills, drugs, procedures … But do not let your head become frozen. As they say, than to plunder bank, it is better to find to itself work.
