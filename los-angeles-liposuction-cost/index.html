Los Angeles Liposuction Cost

Liposuction stably enters the TOP-5 most popular operations in the world. With her help, you can get rid of fat deposits in places, where diet and exercise can not cope with them.

These are the so-called “fat traps”, “pads”, “ears” on the sides and lower back, as well as the climacteric (“widow”) hump and so on.

Often, liposuction is used to improve the body in conjunction with other surgical procedures.

Indications for liposuction:

– A small overweight in the region of the thighs, abdomen, chin and other zones;
– Insufficient results of diets and sports for body correction;
– Age changes in the body associated with weight gain.

How much this procedure is suitable for solving the problems that have arisen with a figure can be clarified by consulting a plastic surgeon.

In our Los angeles clinic, the surgeon’s consultation is free of charge and includes the following stages:

  • The surgeon will answer the client’s questions;
  • Demonstration of the photo “before-after” of similar cases;
  • Computer visualization of results.

Liposuction: preparation for surgery

Before performing a surgical procedure, the patient needs to undergo a series of examinations:

  • To have the general tests;
  • U/S;
  • If necessary, consult a narrow-profile physician.

All this can be done in one day on the basis of our clinic just before liposuction.

Liposuction: features of the procedure

Depending on the appointment of the surgeon, liposuction can be performed using local or general anesthesia. If the surgical procedure is performed using general anesthesia, then within 24 hours after it, you should be in the clinic under the supervision of specialists. For this purpose we have comfortable wards with everything necessary for comfortable staying.

The operating room is equipped with premium equipment, there is resuscitation equipment. Suction of fat cells occurs in a vacuum way through small incisions (up to 0.196in), which quickly heal and do not leave a scar. Responsive clinic staff will support you at all stages of recovery.

Liposuction in Los angeles: recovery period

  • After 7-10 days, the stitches are removed.
  • During the 4-6 weeks required to wear compression underwear.
  • During the month it is not recommended to visit the baths, saunas, beaches, swimming pools.
  • The result is preserved for life, but does not exclude weight gain in other areas where the procedure was not carried out.
