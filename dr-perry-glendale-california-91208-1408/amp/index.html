Liposuction – Am I required to get Body Tite too? What are the pros and cons?
BodyTite
Apr 19, 2018
Typically if you are under 55 your skin still has elasticity, so Liposuction alone gives a great result. BodyTite as an addition to liposuction aids in tightening the skin and promoting collagen production. If your doctor wants to add BodyTite to your liposuction procedure I would assume the addition will give you a firm smooth result.
