About the procedure

Laser liposuction is an innovative technology that was developed by DEKA and has been actively used in Europe and America since 2002. It is based on the principle of selective photoperhytermy, that is, the laser’s ability to selectively affect the cells of adipose tissue, causing their heating and destruction.

The released lysis products are excreted from the body naturally. Therefore, the procedure can be performed without aspiration in such delicate areas as the chin, shoulders, arms, inner thighs.

How is laser liposuction performed?

In Georgia clinic, the liposuction procedure is performed on SmartLipo from DEKA, which developed and patented the liposuction correction technology for the neodymium Nd: YAG laser with a wavelength of 1064 nm.

For liposuction of the abdomen or other areas of the body, a laser optic is introduced through the thin cannula through the patient’s skin, through which radiation is delivered to the places where fat tissue accumulates.

When liposuction is carried out, the radiation is transformed into thermal energy. As a result, the membranes of fat cells are destroyed, and the contents enter the blood and are eliminated naturally.
