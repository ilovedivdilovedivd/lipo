Liposuction in Miami is a method modeling of the body, popular in modern plastic surgery. This is a simple and effective way to create perfect shapes. During surgery, excess fat is removed from the problem area through thin tubes with a vacuum device. The result is a slender body in just one day.

No non-surgical method of losing weight will not give such a quick result. Diets, fitness and tablets do not always help, while after liposuction, fat cells no longer recover.

Water-jet assisted liposuction

Plastic surgeons agree that the safest and most effective method of liposuction is water jet. In contrast to the “high-temperature” technologies (laser and ultrasonic), this kind does not cause complications.

The operation passes quickly and practically painless without burning the skin and internal organs. Another advantage of water-jet liposuction is high productivity. At a time surgeon can remove about 1.58 gallon of fat.

How is the procedure performed?

Water-jet liposuction is carried out without incisions – scars will not remain on the skin after the operation. The removal of fat takes place under general (and in some cases under local) anesthesia. The doctor makes small punctures, through which fat is sucked.

Recovery period for Body Jet Lipo?

The next day after surgery, the patient can leave the clinic. In the presence of small edema and fever, the doctor prescribes painkillers.

3-7 days after surgery – discomfort disappears. The patient can lead a familiar life and start working.
3-6 weeks after the operation – swelling and other residual events completely disappear. The use of compression linen in combination with decongestion procedures will accelerate recovery.
6-8 weeks after the operation – the result becomes more noticeable. Damaged, but not removed from the body of the remains of fat cells gradually die off.

Contraindications

  • Diabetes.
  • Oncological diseases.
  • Diseases of the heart and blood vessels.
  • Blood clotting disorders.
  • Exacerbations of infectious diseases.
  • Mental disorders.
