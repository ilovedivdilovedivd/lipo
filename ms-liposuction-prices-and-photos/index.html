Mississippi Liposuction Prices And Photos

Many people, especially women, are not always happy with their bodies.

They torment themselves with debilitating diets, take medicine, do various special exercises, but the result is not always expectant or short-lived.

And then comes the decision to get rid of ugly parts of the body by liposuction.

Until recently, this procedure was impossible without surgical intervention with a not very easy postoperative period.

But modern scientists have developed and implemented an alternative method of non-surgical liposuction, called – cavitation.

What is the method of non-surgical liposuction?

Cavitation is a revolutionary modern method of removing fat cells under the influence of certain, strictly technologically selected ultrasound waves.

This is an effective, painless, bloodless method that does not have the opposite results.

The technology of the procedure is that ultrasound in the middle of the fat cell creates bubbles that burst, releasing energy that permanently destroys the fatty cage, which means that in this place the fat will never be deposited.

Destroyed fat cells, take the form of fluid, enter the intercellular space and are removed from the body by the lymphatic system.

The result of this method is immediately apparent. The thickness of the fat layer decreases from 1 to 4 cm a few hours after the procedure.
Cavitation provides not only correction of the figure, but also helps to overcome cellulite.

Orange peel with cellulite is not squeezed out, but completely destroyed. The skin in the places where there was cellulite, becomes more elastic and smooth.

Indications for non-surgical liposuction in Mississippi

To remove fatty tissue by the method of cavitation it is possible for all healthy people of any age who do not have contraindications to remove fat by the operating method. With the help of the cavitation procedure, fat deposits are removed in places such as:

  1. waist;
  2. stomach;
  3. buttocks;
  4. thighs;
  5. zone “riding breeches”;
  6. back;
  7. the posterior surface of the neck;
  8. shoulders;
  9. forearms, arms;
  10. knees.

Contra-indications for non-surgical liposuction

Contraindication to cavitation is:

  • liver disease, as broken fat cells are absorbed into the blood and excreted by the liver;
  • chronic kidney disease;
  • cholelithiasis;
  • pregnancy, the period of breastfeeding;
  • disorders of the immune and circulatory systems;
  • insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus;
  • malignant, benign formations;
  • presence of a violation of the skin in the place of the procedure;
  • presence of scars, scars, tattoos in the venue;
  • presence of implants, metal parts in the cavitation zone;
  • osteoporosis;
  • Implants joints;
  • pacemakers;
  • umbilical hernia;
  • menstrual cycle.

Do not allow direct influence of ultrasound on solid tissues, this can lead to their destruction.

Recommendations on preparing for cavitation

For three days before the procedure, you should not drink alcohol, fatty, fried foods, smoked meat, pickles, so as not to put stress on the liver or kidneys. Drink at least 1.5 liters of fluid.

A few hours before the procedure, you need to drink up to a liter of clean water. These recommendations should be followed during the entire course of procedures. After each procedure for several days, you need to drink a liter of water to speed up the removal of breakdown products.

The technology of the procedure and the duration of the course

During the procedure, the surgeon acts on two pairs of areas approximately 15×25 cm long. The duration of one procedure is about 40 minutes. All subsequent cavitation procedures can be done no earlier than seven days.

In parallel with cavitation, it is recommended to perform lymph drainage. The full course of procedures is from 3 to 10 sessions, depending on the degree of obesity. If there is a need to conduct a repeat course of procedures, then you can do this not earlier than six months.
