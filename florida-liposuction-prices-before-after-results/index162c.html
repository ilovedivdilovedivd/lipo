Florida Liposuction Prices & Before After Results

Liposuction in recent years has become widespread due to the use of innovative modern technologies. It is a method of correcting the body by excision of excess subcutaneous fat.

The most problematic areas are the buttock area, the outer thighs, the inner surface of the knees, the flanks, the chin, the abdomen and so on.

A large amount of adipose tissue accumulates in these parts of the body, which can not be removed with fitness training or dietary nutrition.

Aspirating lipectomy will bring the desired result if the excess fatty tissue is not caused by diseases of the internal organs. Another condition for the success of the operation is a good elasticity of the skin.

The operation is not performed for violations of the liver and kidney function, thyroid gland, varicose veins, diabetes, arterial hypersthenia. Any surgical manipulations are contraindicated in infectious, inflammatory processes.

Techniques for conducting

Liposuction can be performed under general or local anesthesia. The surgeon introduces into the pre-designated area a special solution, which includes an anesthetic, vasoconstrictor and electrolytes.

Aspiration of fat is carried out by means of a vacuum pump and cannulae with a diameter of less than 5 mm.

The metal catheter has rounded ends, so the nerve endings and vessels are not damaged. The incisions of the tissues under the standard procedure for length do not exceed 5 mm.

After doing all the manipulations, cosmetic sutures are applied. The duration of the operation depends on the amount of work, varies from 30 minutes to 3-5 hours.

Recovery

  • Within 3-4 days there are painful sensations, puffiness in the area of exposure, perhaps slight malaise, fever.
  • It is recommended to wear special compression underwear to prevent bruising and sagging of the skin.
  • For the purpose of early healing of wounds, physiotherapeutic procedures can be prescribed.
  • During rehabilitation, physical activity is restricted.
  • It is necessary to exclude the use of alcohol.
  • To the habitual rhythm of life, the patient returns after 2-3 weeks.

Possible complications:

  • bleeding,
  • formation of hematomas, seroma,
  • an allergic reaction to anesthesia,
  • The removal of fat deposits can provoke the asymmetry of the area in which the manipulations were performed,
  • decreased skin elasticity,
  • Violation of sensitivity.

In Florida clinic, every possible measure is taken to reduce the likelihood of complications and achieve the most positive cosmetic effect. The final result after the procedure the patient can evaluate after 1-2 months.
