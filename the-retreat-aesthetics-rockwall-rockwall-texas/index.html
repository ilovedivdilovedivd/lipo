If I don’t wear my compression will my results not come out as good?
compression after liposuction
Mar 8, 2019
I generally suggest 4-6 weeks, unless skin laxity is a concern and then I suggest wearing for at least 3 months. It definitely helps to shape in the post operative period. For patients who hate the compression (most patients!), I offer a compromise of wearing 12 hours 3-4 days out of the week once they get to the 4-6 week stage. Hope this helps!
Did I ruin my 24 year old skin after microneedling?
Skin after microneedle
Mar 1, 2019
Your skin is likely inflamed from the procedure. Remember with microneedling and many things in aesthetic medicine, we are creating an injury to force the body to “Reset” and create new, healthier skin. There is a possibility you’re allergic to a topical product used in conjunction with the procedure if it’s overly inflamed. I would follow up with your esthetician regardless or find one you trust locally. Good luck!
