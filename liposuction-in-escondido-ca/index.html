Liposuction In Escondido, CA

The technique, according to which fat deposits are surgically removed, is called liposuction.

Fat is more often deposited on the hips, abdomen and waist, internal surfaces of the knees, less often – on the cheeks, chin, neck and shoulders.

However, it should be understood that liposuction in Escondido is not a method of treating general obesity.

For the operation is appropriate that category of patients who have single disproportionate fat deposits, poorly amenable to diet and exercise.

Often, the procedure is performed in conjunction with abdominoplasty.

On consultation at the surgeon

To determine who and where to do liposuction, you need to not only make inquiries on the Internet and friends, but also make an appointment for a consultation with a plastic surgeon. During the conversation the doctor finds out from the patient:

  1. Body weight and how it has changed over the past 5-10 years.
  2. A patient’s habitual lifestyle, does he engage in sports.
  3. The patient’s profession.
  4. What diet and exercise were used to reduce body weight.
  5. Have you ever had liposuction or other surgery in problem areas.
  6. If the patient has bleeding disorders, liver diseases.
  7. What medicines are taken, including vitamins and supplements.
  8. What effect does the patient expect from the procedure.

All these questions are of great importance for the surgeon, so be prepared to give specific answers to them.

During the conversation, you will understand whether you need to do liposuction or not, whether there are contraindications, get information about the main points of the operation and the idea of the clinic and the treating doctor.

Methods of liposuction in Escondido

Liposuction is carried out by many centers of plastic surgery, since this service is in demand among the population. Medicine does not stand still, and today there is not one way to do liposuction. Vacuum liposuction is the very first proposed method of correction of the body shape.

It is performed under general anesthesia. The doctor enters the cannula (hollow needles) into the subcutaneous fat tissue and mechanically destroys the fat cells, which are removed through the connected vacuum aspirator.

The procedure is traumatic and is accompanied by blood loss, which may require even blood transfusion therapy (recovery of circulating blood volume) in the postoperative period.

Tumescent liposuction in Escondido is a modification of the vacuum method. The difference is that before pumping fat, tissue impregnated with a special fluid consisting of saline, adrenaline and lidocaine. Vessels are narrowed and the risk of their damage during surgery is minimal.

The fatty tissue loosens, and in this condition it is easier to remove through thin cannulas. Such a procedure is possible under local anesthesia.

Syringe liposuction can be performed in areas where a small amount of fat is removed, for example, on the cheeks. Due to the technique, the operation is performed longer, but traces from punctures are not noticeable.

If the patient is afraid of surgical interventions, then it is possible to perform liposuction without surgery. For this purpose, techniques such as ultrasonic, laser and electronic liposuction have been developed. Their disadvantage is that it is difficult in this way to remove a large amount of fat.

When is it better to do liposuction?

We can say that liposuction is a method of despair. This kind of plastic is used to get rid of local fat deposits. It’s not about treating obesity. Therefore, before you do liposuction, adjust your diet, increase motor activity. Perhaps these simple methods will suffice.

Repeated liposuction

Those who did liposuction, sometimes remain dissatisfied with the result. The reason for this may be insufficient fat removal.

Such patients can undergo a repeat procedure, but only after 6 months, when the deep tissue will heal and the swelling will go away. However, repeated liposuction is more difficult in technical terms and worse in terms of prognosis.

How much does liposuction cost?

The answer to the question “how much does liposuction cost?” Will depend on the area of operation, the surgeon’s choice of technique and the amount of surgical intervention. There are three degrees:

  • Liposuction of small volume (removal up to 2.5 liters of fat).
  • Liposuction of large volume (removal of 2.5-5 liters of fat).
  • Liposuction of extra-large volume (removal of more than 5 liters of fat).

Before the operation, the surgeon inserts a solution containing a local anesthetic and a vasoconstrictor. This reduces the risk of bleeding. Fat cells under the action of the solution swell and increase in volume. Fat from the solid state is converted to liquid, so its amount is measured in gallons, not in pounds. The surgeon may not carry out too voluminous operations, but break it down into several stages. Thus, it may take several treatments and the expected cost will increase significantly.

Usually patients are satisfied with the quality of the results achieved. Therefore, we do not see the need to use other more expensive methods that have rather controversial advantages.
