Liposuction In Bend Oregon

Liposuction in Bend is known in plastic surgery for more than three decades. This revolutionary method of removing excess fat has opened up real new possibilities in plastic surgery.

Today in the world plastic surgeons perform so many liposuction operations that it rightly bears the title of the most popular cosmetic procedure.

What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a surgical operation, the purpose of which is vacuum suction of excess fatty tissue under the skin. Liposuction is performed using a long hollow tube, called a cannula. This tube is inserted through a small incision-puncture under the skin and connected to a vacuum apparatus.

Who needs liposuction and whether it is necessary to you?

Liposuction, as already mentioned, is performed to remove excess fat from the subcutaneous fat. Therefore, it is indicated to people with excess fatty tissue, when it hangs down in the form of folds on the body and in other areas, such as the abdomen, thighs, arms, neck.

Excess fat is found not only in people who do not follow a diet and lead a sedentary lifestyle. Fat can appear in those who regularly perform physical exercises and try to avoid fatty foods.

In this case, everything is connected with the genetic predisposition of the person himself, as well as a violation of metabolism and other factors.

But despite the fact that liposuction is aimed at removing excess fat, it is not suitable for all fat people. The ideal candidate for liposuction is a physically developed patient who regularly exercises, while he does not have more than 19 lb of excess weight.

That is, the less you have excess fat, the better will be the results of liposuction. People with the same obesity at once remove all fat with liposuction is simply dangerous, so they may need several sessions of this procedure.

Note also the fact that immediately after liposuction is not recommended to sit on a strict diet. Liposuction is not a panacea for obesity.

The best results this operation gives in those cases when patients made efforts to lose excess fat by means of a diet and exercises.

That’s when no exercises and diets can help to remove fat from the abdomen, thighs, etc., then liposuction comes to the rescue.

Contraindications to liposuction in Bend OR

Strangely enough, even to such a seemingly simple procedure as liposuction, there are contraindications. Immediately it is necessary to say that liposuction is not done for the purpose of reducing weight! It is more likely to be done to correct the body.

Weight is reduced only by diet in combination with exercise. In some cases, weight reduction is achieved with the help of bariatric surgery, but not liposuction. So, remember – you can not lose weight with liposuction!

That is why liposuction is not suitable for patients with very large excess weight or large body fat. Liposuction is possible only after they can reduce excess weight. Again, this requires diet and intense physical activity.

If the reduction in excess weight through dieting and physical activity for the patient is difficult, then a procedure such as “gastric bypass” can be proposed. The principle of this operation is that the surgeon divides the stomach into a small upper part and a large lower part.

As a result, the functional volume of the stomach decreases. The patient does not eat superfluous, which in turn can not but affect the reduction of excess weight.

Liposuction in Bend OR is also used in cases when, after intensive weight loss, the patient has excess skin areas. Such skin ugly dangles in the form of folds, on the abdomen, hips, arms, neck, etc.

In this case, a lifting (body lifting) is carried out, for example, abdominoplasty (tummy tuck technique) to strengthen the abdominal wall, and at the same time it is possible to perform liposuction to reduce body fat.

After bariatric surgery, the patient may have a so-called fat apron. This is a dangling fold of the skin that formed after weight loss. The solution may be an operation of pannicullectomy.
