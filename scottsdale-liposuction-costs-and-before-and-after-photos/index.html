Scottsdale Liposuction Costs And Before And After Photos

The essence of liposuction is the surgical correction of the outline of the body due to the removal of fat deposits. With the help of vacuum suction through the small incisions of the skin eliminate local deposits of fat.

Indications for liposuction:

– The local form of fat deposits. Most often these are areas of the outer and inner surfaces of the thighs, knees, lower legs, abdomen, back, lumbar and chin areas.
– In the generalized form of fat deposits, liposuction is used as an additional weight loss method in conjunction with conservative methods.
– As an additional method for abdominoplasty, creating mobility for the skin flap.
– Aesthetic dissatisfaction with body shapes.

Contraindications for liposuction:

  • Diseases of the cardiovascular system
  • Diseases of the blood
  • Oncological diseases
  • Pregnancy
  • Skin diseases
  • Diabetes

What is included in the cost of the liposuction surgery:

  • Accommodation in a private ward;
  • Inspection of anesthesiologist;
  • Anesthesia;
  • Operation;
  • Dynamic monitoring of the patient in the postoperative period.

Liposuction is used, if there are local forms of obesity with violation of the outline of the body (most often in women there is a deformation of the hips – so-called cellulite, fat deposits on the inner side surface of the knee joint, deposits in the thigh, anterior and lateral surfaces of the abdomen.

Also, liposuction is used for the accumulation of adipose tissue in the abdominal area and good elasticity of the skin. With the accumulation of adipose tissue in the chin, liposuction improves the contour of the neck and can be performed simultaneously with the removal of wrinkles in the face and neck area or as a separate operation.

Liposuction has been used successfully to remove lipomas – benign tumors of adipose tissue. Liposuction, like any cosmetic procedure or operation, has limitations.

The nearest results of liposuction are evaluated after 1.5 months after the tissue state is normalized.

During this period, the healing of deeper tissues takes place, the disappearance of puffiness, the contraction of the skin.

Different people have this process in different ways: one for a full recovery takes a little more time, the other less.

What tests should I pass before a plastic surgery?

  • Blood type, Rh factor;
  • Clinical blood test, clotting time, bleeding time, platelets – valid for 10 days;
  • Coagulogram – 10 days;
  • RW, HBs Ag, anti HCV, HIV – valid for 3 months;
  • Bilirubin, total protein, glucose, urea, creatinine -10 days;
  • ECG – 10 days;
  • Clinical analysis of urine – 10 days;
  • fluorography.

Does anesthesia affect the heart and the body as a whole?

Properly conducted anesthesia is safe for the heart and other systems and organs. Our clinic uses only modern drugs that do not have harmful effects on the body.

Is plastic surgery painful?

The level of modern anesthesiology is such that in any operation absolutely complete anesthesia is provided. If the patient is aware of his individual intolerance to some or other anesthetic drugs, then it is necessary to inform the surgeon in advance.
