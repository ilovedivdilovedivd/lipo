Does Jeuveau require more units than Dysport?
Jeauveau vs. Dysport
Nov 18, 2023
Jeauveau and Dysport units are measured differently. For every one unit of Jeaveau you need to use three units of Dysport. So, your old Dysport dose was the equivalent of 20 units of Jeuveau (10 on each side).
