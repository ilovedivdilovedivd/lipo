Uneven areolas after breast augmentation. What are my revision options?
Breast lift with implant and nipple repositioning
Jan 14, 2021
Hello! It appears that loss of the right inframammary fold (where the breast meets the chest) integrity has allowed inferior implant migration, giving the illusion of the right nipple having areola elevation.
Options include repositioning a right breast implant and then consideration of a bilateral mastopexy (breast lift) – to restore the shape of both breasts – with nipple repositioning, pending the final position of both nipple areola complexes.
Smooth or textured implants under the muscle?
Smooth implants
Jan 14, 2021
At present, I do not recommend textured implants at any site due to the risk of Anaplastic large cell lymphoma (ALCL). However, bottoming out can occur with either implant shell type. It may be a result of an individual anatomic issue or a technical issue during the surgical procedure.
Do you think I will look worse if I only have liposuction?
A tummy tuck would be ideal to tighten the skin.
Jan 14, 2021
An abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) will provide the best skin appearance. If you prefer a more rapid recovery or smaller surgical scar, options could include: ultrasound assisted liposuction or radio frequency assisted liposuction (BodyTite). The results would be limited with only a liposuction; you’d need something to tighten the skin.
