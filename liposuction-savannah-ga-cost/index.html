Liposuction Savannah GA Cost

Liposuction in Savannah is an operation to destroy fat tissues and then remove them from the body.

The main purpose of the intervention is visual correction of the body.

Removal of excess fatty tissue can radically and in a short period of time improve the outline of the body or face oval.

In particular, this method is used for correction:

– hips;
– abdomen;
– buttocks;
– cheeks;
– chin.

It should be remembered that liposuction is a local solution to the problem, which does not exclude the development of obesity in the future.

Vibro-liposuction

Vibro-liposuction is an invention of Belgian specialists. Through thin cannulas, compressed air enters the fat cells and instantly destroys them, and the formed adipocyte residues are removed by the vacuum device.

This is a very gentle and less traumatic method, which is suitable for delicate areas.

Tumescent method

The surgeon makes small incisions on the skin of the problem zones and inserts the cannulae, through which the sodium chloride solution, lidocaine, adrenaline enters.

Under the influence of these substances, fat cells are gradually destroyed, as well as local anesthesia.

The method is characterized by a short rehabilitation period (3-4 days), no blood loss and no noticeable bruising after surgery.

LASER LIPOSUCTION

This method was presented to us by Italy. The laser destroys the fat cells, and also coagulates the vessels, which minimizes the risk of infection, the formation of hematomas and swelling.

RADIOFREQUENCY LIPOSUCTION

This costly procedure has the following advantages:

  1. zero chance of burns;
  2. minimum recovery period;
  3. absence of visible traces of interference;
  4. a noticeable lifting effect.

Fat cells are destroyed under the influence of high-frequency current. Internal and external electrodes are used, and the procedure is performed using expensive equipment and under the constant supervision of a specialist. But if there are contraindications to classical surgical methods, this is an excellent alternative.

Ultrasound probe

Allows you to remove about 7 liters of fat in one session. The ultrasound probe is inserted under the skin through a small incision and provides a smooth skin, as well as a stable result.

Whatever method of getting rid of boring pounds and “orange peel” you choose, remember that the most important thing is to choose a good specialist. And it is also important to follow the instructions regarding the preparation for the operation and the recovery period. This is a full-fledged surgical intervention, which must be treated with all seriousness.

On our body there are certain areas on which the subcutaneous fat “loves” to accumulate.

Wise nature specially singled out these zones in order to create in them a kind of energy reserve in case of unforeseen circumstances (for example, pregnancy). But sometimes this stock becomes redundant and spoils the figure.

In this case, modern surgical methods, pharmacological preparations and even subconscious programming come to the aid of women. The most common method of removing excess body fat is liposuction.

The first operation to “suction” fat doctors held in the early 20th century. But, unfortunately, it was unsuccessful, and as a result, work in this area was resumed only in the last decades of the last century. Over time, liposuction has become one of the most common and relatively safe operations that plastic surgeons do almost daily.

There are several types of liposuction: laser, tumescent, standard, vibromechanical, ultrasonic and syringe. The latter is the most sparing, but it is only used when correcting small problem areas.

The operation is performed according to the same principle: adipose tissue destroyed by ultrasound (laser, vibromechanical) is evacuated from the subcutaneous space with the help of special cannula tubules. Liposuction is usually performed in a hospital and under general anesthesia.

After the operation, you will have to wear special compression clothes. The end result can be admired only after 4-6 months.

Contraindications for liposuction are oncological diseases, heart problems and diabetes. The operation is also contraindicated in the period of exacerbation of chronic ailments.
