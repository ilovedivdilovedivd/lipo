Common Myths

The clinic of plastic surgery has doctors in its staff who will tell you in detail about the peculiarities and subtleties of this operation. But we will get acquainted with the widespread myths right now.

Myth number 1:

After surgery, the total body weight decreases. Yes, belly liposuction, the prices for which are available to most people, really allows you to get rid of fat cells in this area. But it does not help to reduce the total body weight. You can get rid of hated pounds only in case of proper nutrition and regular exercise. Although many do not help this. In this case, plastic surgeons come to the aid of people.

Myth number 2:

Liposuction of the waist (photos before and after presented in the catalogs of the clinics) removes the flabbiness of the skin. No no and one more time no! Liposuction removes fat cells, but it does not get rid of the flabby skin that appears after the operation. If you want it to be elastic, use also a tummy tuck.

So, surgically removes excess skin on the abdomen. Sometimes lift tightens the abdominal muscles. Of course, thanks to the operation, the treated areas of the body are smoothed out, they acquire a contour, but for a perfect result, constant physical exertion is required.
