Timing for second Sculpsure treatment. How long should i wait?
Timing for second SculpSure treatment
Jun 27, 2018
Cynosure recommends you wait at least 6 weeks between SculpSure treatments. There are cases where patients experience swelling and/or nodules post treatment and it is important that this has resolved before the second treatment. We typically recommend our patients wait at least 8 weeks in between treatments. You should see improvement in fat reduction for up to 12 weeks. Glad to hear you are pleased with the results from you first SculpSure treatment!
