Liposuction is a reduction, through individual skin punctures, the volume of adipose tissue in the subcutaneous basis.

The homeland of liposuction is France. Dr. Illouz first conducted this operation in the 70’s.

Since then, this technique has gained unprecedented popularity all over the world and is on the heights of all sorts of ratings among the methods of plastic surgery.

Naturally, this dynamically developing section of plastic surgery constantly undergoes improvements and modifications.

There are various methods of liposuction, but the most effective for today are laser and ultrasound liposuction.

The top of the evolution of methods for removing superfluous subcutaneous fat is undoubtedly ultrasonic liposuction, in which the fat tissue under the influence of ultrasound is converted into an emulsion and immediately pumped out under the influence of a vacuum.
The advantages of ultrasonic liposuction are small tissue trauma, no bleeding, shortening, tightening of the skin (additional effect), rapid wound healing, less bruising and edema, shorter overall and aesthetic rehabilitation, the possibility of applying liposuction without risk in all areas of the face, body and limbs.

Our specialists have undergone training on ultrasonic liposuction from the best specialists of the USA and Europe.
