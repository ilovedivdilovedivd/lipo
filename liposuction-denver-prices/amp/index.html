Liposuction Denver Prices

Removal of excess adipose tissue on the trunk, extremities, face by aspiration.

Who would have thought that the initial attempts to remove fat surgically with the help of vacuum liposuction were back in 1974.

It was then that the Italian doctor G. Fisher removed the fat tissue to the patient through the incisions, using a rotating scalpel.

Fisher’s instrument, was a tube with a blade built into it, and provided a mechanical unification of fat.

The scalpel destroyed the fat cells, which were then removed from the body through vacuum suction.

Unfortunately, such an intervention was very traumatic, and led to the fact that patients lost a large amount of blood.

In our time, the operation of liposuction in Denver is the title of the most popular cosmetic procedure. And if many years ago this operation had a negative effect on the body and provoked all sorts of consequences, now everything has changed radically. It is safe to say that liposuction today is quite an effective and safe procedure, allowing women and men to be always in shape.

What is the essence of liposuction and what is its uniqueness? Liposuction is an operation during which, through punctures on the skin with the help of needles “cannula” and a vacuum aspirator, excess fat is removed.

To date, the relevance of liposuction is difficult to underestimate. Unlike the traditional methods of fighting obesity, liposuction makes it possible to quickly and permanently get rid of excess weight in problem areas. In addition, it is worth adding that by aspiration you can also improve the shape of the face.

Methods of liposuction

With the advent of new technologies, liposuction methods do not cease to improve. Among the main methods is to distinguish classical, ultrasound, syringe liposuction, lipomodeling.

During standard liposuction, small incisions are performed through which a special needle (cannula) is inserted. Then, slowly moving the doctor directs the needle under the skin, destroying the fat cells. After this method, as a result of healing, a framework of fibrous tissue is formed, which prevents the growth of the fat layer and compacts it.

Syringe liposuction is used when you need to remove very little fat deposits. During this operation, the specialist uses a syringe instead of vacuum pumps. The essence of the operation is that with the syringe a needle is inserted into the fat accumulation zone, after which the specialist pulls the piston, thus creating the necessary negative pressure. In turn, the syringe serves as a liposuction.

During the ultrasonic method, fat is removed by ultrasonic waves. Under the influence of ultrasound, the fat tissue is converted into a liquid emulsion, which is subsequently removed by a vacuum method.

Electronic lipomodelling is often called a real sensation in the world of liposuction. This method is based on the effect of the current dissolving adipose tissue. Such modeling is considered the most sparing of all the existing methods of liposuction.

The specialists of the clinic will select for the most optimal method of aspiration based on the individual characteristics of the patient.
