5'4, 130lbs, ticklish stomach after lipo 1 year ago, is it going to go away?
Sensory Nerves
Jul 5, 2023
Just know that you're not alone when it comes to unusual sensations after a procedure. Everyone's body responds to surgery, or trauma, differently. In fact, your body can't tell the difference between trauma and (cosmetic) surgery). Often times, nerve regeneration (specifically sensory nerves) can give the feeling of burning, zapping, or an uncomfortable feeling. This feeling, however, typically resolves by 12 months post-surgery, on average. Hope this helps. -Dr. Sean Satey, Beverly Hills, CA.
