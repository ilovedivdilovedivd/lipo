What is laser liposuction?

One of the varieties of liposuction in Honolulu is a laser, interest in which is shown by full and thin women who have excess fat deposits in various parts of the body. Laser liposuction is very common in developed countries, where the ladies already appreciated its advantages and were satisfied with the results. Fat cells are removed by laser energy.

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia or general anesthesia is used. The type of anesthesia depends on the amount of fat to be removed.

The method of laser liposuction is not complicated and safe if the procedure is performed by an experienced doctor. The working tool in the hands of a professional is an optic-fibrous very thin laser probe. Having made a small puncture, the doctor inserts under the skin a thin hollow cannula, the diameter of which does not exceed 1 millimeter.

Then a probe is injected into the cannula, emitting laser energy, which realizes the process of lipolysis, which damages fat cells. From the destroyed cells, fat enters the bloodstream and, together with blood, passes into the liver, where it is naturally neutralized.

This method of fat elimination is especially attractive not only because it allows you to adjust the silhouette, but also in the sense that the laser stimulates the production of collagen, so important for the skin and improving its appearance.

How to prepare for the procedure of laser liposuction?

The procedure for laser liposuction should be planned, rather than taking spontaneous decisions. In a good clinic you will not only have an explanatory conversation, but will also be asked to take tests to assess your current condition.

We briefly describe what needs to be done or what should be abandoned when planning laser liposuction.

Refuse to take medications. For more information about medications that should be taken away, you will receive a doctor who will prepare you for the operation.

Refuse from smoking. Since it is not always easy to give up this habit, start planning liposuction in a few months.

Smoking can not be a few weeks after the operation of liposuction, because smoking has a negative effect on the healing process. On the eve of the procedure, take a relaxing warm bath. Keep in mind that from such procedures, exactly as from visiting the bath, sauna and pool should be abandoned at the time of recovery.

Your actions after liposuction

Having gone through such a simple and safe laser liposuction, the patient will be under medical supervision for about an hour. If after this time the doctor does not reveal any violations and assess the client’s condition as satisfactory, the patient will be able to go home.

  1. After the procedure, exercise should be removed for 2 weeks. Do not forget that you need to step back from visiting the sauna, bath and pool. Briefly describe the main recommendations that should be followed.
  2. For 2 weeks, give up sports and exercise.
  3. Wear a bandage on the area where the procedure was performed. If it is a question of the face or chin – it is enough not to remove the bandage for 2 days, if it is a question of other parts of the body – the bandage should not be removed for 7 days.
  4. 5 days after the procedure, take antibiotics to prevent the formation of infectious diseases.
  5. Take care to place the input cannula was clean and dry.
  6. Refuse from drinking alcohol for 12 hours after the operation.
  7. After a week and a half, do a little very light massage in the part of the body where the laser procedure was performed. An energetic massage will be forbidden for you during 5 months after the procedure.
  8. Do not forget that with the appearance of edema and painful sensations, you should immediately inform the doctor who will take appropriate measures.
