Prices Of Liposuction In Austin TX

Liposuction is a correction of fat traps to improve the contours of the body. It can be surgical and non-surgical.

The greatest effect is achieved with surgical liposuction, which involves small incisions or punctures on the skin. Depending on the therapeutic factor used, there are several types of surgical liposuction: vacuum, laser, radio frequency and others.

Vacuum liposuction, in turn, is divided into tumescent, wet and classic dry.

The specialists of Austin clinic have a great experience in removing fat tissue from any problem zone – the abdomen, back, mammary glands, the “riding breeches”, knees, hands and other areas of the body. The most frequently performed tumescent liposuction.

The Klein solution is injected into the area of the fat trap, wait a few minutes, and then the fat is removed by means of a vacuum device through thin cannulas. With minimal trauma, significant areas can be treated with up to 11lb of adipose tissue removed, while the elastic properties of the skin do not suffer.

An important advantage of the method consists in the fact that fat is excreted from the body at the time of the procedure, without subsequent loading on the liver and kidneys.

After removing large amounts of adipose tissue, special procedures are required to correct the skin folds that have formed. Tumescent liposuction is often combined with plastic surgery – for example, with abdominoplasty, breast surgery.

Other types of surgical and non-surgical liposuction have their own characteristics, their advantages. The doctor will tell you which method suits you the most.

FAQ

Does liposuction help with a “double chin”?

If there is an excess of adipose tissue in the chin and the skin is elastic, then liposuction is very effective. However, if the skin has lost elasticity and become flabby, then one liposuction will not be enough. Patients with flabby skin usually need rhytidectomy in addition to or in lieu of liposuction.

As practice shows, in patients under 40 years old, one liposuction is often enough to achieve the desired result. Patients over 40 years of age often need additional surgical manipulations, at least in the same facelift and neck lift.

Is it necessary to lose weight before liposuction?

No! As a result, suction is much more difficult! Wait, please, with weight loss before liposuction (then losing weight, as a rule, is much easier). In addition, liposuction performed before weight reduction, is conducive, as a rule, to the recovery of “excess” skin, so that the risk of the appearance of hanging folds of the skin is reduced.

How Will Fat Return After Liposuction?

In order to place as much fat tissue on the site of liposuction as it was before the operation, it will take several tens of years.

But this operation, of course, does not normalize the fat metabolism, and if after it does not watch for yourself, the fat will be deposited on other parts of the body.

Myths about liposuction

Myths are an integral part of our life, probably there is no such area or subject around which there would not be many misconceptions, and liposuction was not an exception.

With the help of liposuction, as many believe, you can not fight obesity, the causes of which lie in the problems of the endocrine system. Of course, this is the only method for quickly getting rid of excess fatty tissue, but it is also recommended to observe the endocrinologist.

After liposuction, the skin condition worsens significantly, this is another myth. In fact, the skin can contract slowly, but an experienced surgeon necessarily minimizes such consequences by choosing the optimal method of pumping out fat and the intensity of liposuction, especially since after the procedure for a month, special underwear should be worn that contributes to better cutaneous skin tissue.

After liposuction, you can lead a habitual way of life – this statement is also no more than a myth. If you go back to your previous lifestyle, forget about sports and healthy nutrition, most likely, over time, fat will again find itself in its rightful place.

Liposuction of the abdomen, the cost of which, thanks to the development of technology, is becoming more accessible, is effective only if you turn to a professional and make a decision to radically change your lifestyle, and it really is the only method of removing excess weight with proven effectiveness.
