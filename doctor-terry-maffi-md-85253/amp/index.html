Is laser resurfacing okay to perform during a facelift?
Laser Resurfacing During a Facelift
Nov 20, 2023
Laser resurfacing is very safe to perform at the same time as a facelift. and I do so on a good majority of my facelift patients.
The benefit of this is combining the downtime of each procedure into one and not having to endure the initial pain of the laser like you would if you were to do the laser treatment in the office.
Deep plane SMAS - how does the SMAS get reattached?
SMAS Facelift
Nov 20, 2023
Yes, the SMAS is elevated, suspended and then stitched to help secure it. Strong sutures are used to suture the SMAS to fascia which is a strong casing of connective tissue that surrounds the muscles of the upper face.
Detaching the SMAS after surgery is rare however, limiting activity for approximately 3 to 4 weeks after surgery will help prevent this.

I am hearing that a lot of facelifts don't last past 5 years. Is this true?
The best facelifts last 5-7 years
Nov 20, 2023
Unfortunately, a facelift does not last 15 years. It depends on the age at which you undergo the procedure but in general, a properly done facelift will last approximately 5 to 7 years. An important caveat to mention is that after this time, you are not going to look exactly as you did prior to your facelift. You will still have an improvement, however, after 5 to 7 years you will most likely have some sagging and redundancy that could bother you and you may consider another facelift.
Mini face + neck lift, fat grafting, and upper and lower eyelid surgery - truthful recovery time?
2-3 Months of Recovery Time
Nov 20, 2023
Having this combination of facial surgeries is very common and something that I recommend often to my patients. It will save you multiple recovery times by combining them all into a single surgery day. Typically, I tell patients that it takes approximately 4 weeks to start looking “normal” and 2 to 3 months until you will look your best. If you are on camera, I would suggest waiting 3 months to be fully camera ready.
