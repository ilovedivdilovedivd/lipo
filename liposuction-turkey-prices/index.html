Liposuction Turkey Prices

Liposuction in Turkey is one of the most popular plastic surgery in the world today.

After all, even on a slender body appear insidious fat “traps”, from which do not help to get rid of neither sports, nor a debilitating diet.

What is the cause of body fat? The decisive role in the occurrence of local fat deposits play:

– nutrition and way of life;
– the main genetically determined model of fat distribution;
– gender: in women and men, the distribution of fat tissue occurs in different ways;
– hormonal changes, with this problem of local fat deposits are more often encountered by women: during pregnancy, child feeding and after menopause, there is hormone-induced redistribution of – adipose tissue and often these changes are irreversible.

The most effective way to remove excess fat is liposuction. It is liposuction that helps the patient to correct the imperfections of the body, to achieve a harmonious silhouette.

Therefore, the indication for surgery is the presence of persistent fat deposits. It is important to understand that liposuction is not a method of weight loss, but only allows you to correct the imperfections of the body shape.

What is Liposuction?

Liposuction in Turkey is a plastic surgery for the correction of the figure, during which the removal of surplus subcutaneous fat. Currently, several types of liposuction are performed:

  1. tumescent (tumescent)
  2. ultrasound liposuction
  3. lipopulse (vibration liposuction)
  4. laser
  5. water jet

Preoperative preparation

Preoperative preparation includes examination (tests, ECG, consultation of other specialists, in the presence of concomitant pathology).

Immediately before the plastic surgery, photography of problem areas is carried out in several projections. After that, the zones are marked out, which are subject to correction.

Operation

Liposuction is performed under intravenous anesthesia. A special solution is injected subcutaneously, which provides for easier and more qualitative removal of excess fatty tissue and minimal blood loss.

Further, from small punctures, removal of excess fatty tissue is performed. At the end of the operation, punctures are sutured.

The duration of the operation depends on the number of treated zones, but on average it takes from 1 to 4.5 – 5 hours.

Postoperative period

Immediately after the operation, special compression garment are worn, which must be worn for 4-6 weeks. The next day after the surgery, a dressing is performed and the patient can be discharged home, so after 24 hours you can return to your usual life.

It is necessary to be prepared for the fact that the postoperative period is accompanied by the appearance of bruises and swelling (as a consequence, temporary obstructed lymph drainage) that take place within 2 to 2.5 weeks.

After the operation, the contours of the body improve noticeably, however, due to the edema that inevitably occurs after liposuction, the preliminary result can be judged no earlier than 1.5-2 months, and the final result is estimated 4-6 months after the operation.

Recommendations

During the first month after plastic surgery, you must avoid physical activity, exercise, visiting the sauna, solarium and sunbathing. To reduce and facilitate the rehabilitation period, we recommend a course of lymphatic drainage massage.

Operational risks / complications

We must not forget that liposuction is primarily an operation and as any operation, it has its possible complications:

  • Moderate pain syndrome in the area of liposuction zones, which can last about a week (slightly less or slightly more, depending on the individual threshold of pain sensitivity).
  • Appearance of edema and bruising (bruises), which gradually resolve (up to 2 – 2.5 weeks).
  • Possible local loss of sensitivity (numbness) in the area of liposuction zones, which is gradually restored.
  • In patients with varicose veins of the lower extremities, there is a risk of thrombosis and thromboembolism.
  • Fortunately, this is an extremely rare complication, but it is impossible to exclude it 100%. In this connection, patients who potentially have an increased risk of developing PE (thrombotic pulmonary embolism), additional preventive measures are performed.
  • With age, the elasticity of the skin decreases, and therefore, with a significant amount of removed fat, later, other plastic surgeries (for example, abdominoplasty) may be required.

Exacerbation of chronic diseases by other systems and organs.

Liposuction can be a good incentive for a proper lifestyle and balanced diet. However, it is worthwhile to understand that if these simple rules are not observed, the result from the operation may not be long.
