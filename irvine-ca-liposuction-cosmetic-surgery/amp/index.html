Traces from the liposuction of the abdomen, thighs, knees:

In any zone, wherever lipomodeling is performed, the seam will be hidden in a natural fold. The scar is no more than 3 mm. The technique is almost the same everywhere, whether it’s the stomach, hips or knees. Manipulation can differ only depending on the equipment.

When liposuction of the abdomen, we try to hide the seam in the navel, if the operation is performed on the pubis – then in the femoral fold, there – in the operation on the outer surface of the thighs. As for the liposuction of the knees – then in a natural fold over the kneecap we make a small puncture.

With the correct use of “relief lines” a person can hide any scar. For example, if we make an incision across the contraction of the muscle, then the scar heals well afterwards. If along the contraction of the muscles this will be a rough substandard scar.

Liposuction is also performed in the buttock area. Puncture with such manipulation remains in the gluteal fold. If it’s about liposuction of the cheeks – you can make it from the inner mucous side of the mouth. On the back – depending on which problem zone. The main thing is that an incision is across the muscle contraction.

Unevenness after liposuction, complications

The procedure of liposuction involves a reduction in the thickness of fatty tissue, as a result of which excess skin can appear. In patients under the age of 45, everything is fairly well reduced. But rather, it depends no longer on age, but on the potential of your own skin. The surgeon needs to evaluate the tissue and adequately explain to the patient what may happen in the future.

Even the perfect skin can reduce badly. If this still happened, it is necessary to perform excision of excess skin. If there was just a drop in the inner surface of the thighs, the fat by lipofilling method is transplanted into this zone. You can enter fillers that give a temporary, but good cosmetic effect. The effect of contour plastics persists for 8-9 months.

Possible complications include hematoma and seroma, when fluid is collected in the cavities.

Recovery period after liposuction in Irvine

After the patient has undergone surgery, he remains in the hospital for 1-2 days, then within 1 month the patient needs to wear special compression clothes. Often, the surgeon appoints a course of recovery lymphatic drainage massage, which helps reduce the recovery period.

Most patients after liposuction did return to their habitual way rather quickly, but the final aesthetic result can be estimated only after 2-3 months.

It is important to note, that in the manipulation conducted by any of these methods, up to 1.06 gallon of fat is removed, if the patient wants to correct large body volumes, then the operation in this case is carried out in 2 stages. The key moment in the recovery period is the wearing of compression underwear.
