Hardware liposuction is a procedure in which ultrasonic waves directed at a specific surface of the human body penetrate the skin without injuring it and destroy the fat cells by damaging the integrity of their membranes.

After such attacks, the cells are not able to recover. Their internal content is utilized in a completely natural way, being withdrawn from the body together with other decay products.

Hardware procedures

Destructive waves of ultrasound are produced by the apparatus. In addition to eliminating excess fat, these devices can still improve the skin’s elasticity, causing it to produce more collagen, which automatically leads to an increase in its elasticity.

Basically, one session of hardware liposuction involves both sides of the question: it also destroys the subcutaneous tissue, making the body more slender, and contributes to the fact that the skin becomes more elastic and taut. This versatility is made possible by the possibility of adjusting the required temperature.

When cooling the subcutaneous layers, fat breaks down (this is only natural, since lowering the body temperature provokes the start of the process of fat splitting in order to obtain additional energy, which is directed to normalize its temperature), and when heated, the production of proteins responsible for the elasticity of the skin becomes more active.

To achieve the desired effect, you should take a course of several procedures. Their number, on average, does not amount to less than 6-7. To completely eliminate the defects of the body, which is the cause of accumulated fat, it is recommended that in addition to lipolysis, another session of vacuum-roller massage, mesotherapy, and wrapping is included.

These procedures will speed up the result and make it more durable. The peculiarities of the procedures and their complexes should be discussed with the specialist who conducts lipolysis.
