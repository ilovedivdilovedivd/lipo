The fat cell is one of the few cells in the human body that does not recover.

The number of fat cells in the body stabilizes to about five years, but they can increase in size by several dozen times.

Factors determining the number of fat cells in the body are several:

– Genetic predisposition;
– Correct nutrition of the mother during pregnancy;
– The correctness of feeding the baby in the first months after birth.

If the amount of fat cells in the human body is at the level of the lower boundary, it will almost always be thin and lean.

A man with an average number of fat cells: he does not have much difficulty in gaining weight or losing weight. People with the number of fat cells near the upper border are almost always predisposed to fullness.

In order to avoid excess weight, you need to remember two simple rules: to eat regularly and regularly and lead an active lifestyle. In the world there is a huge number of different diets, but almost all of them have one thing in common: when a person “descends” from a diet, he quickly regains the former or even greater weight.

Liposuction in Atlanta is an operation that removes fat deposits only in certain places (neck, chin, hips, buttocks, stomach).

This aesthetic operation should not be taken as a means of combating obesity, – we treat it differently. Liposuction is designed to remove fat only from problem areas and thereby make the figure more aesthetic and beautiful.

There are several types of liposuction – vacuum, ultrasound and the latter invention – a method of “gentle liposuction” – vibro-liposuction, in which the usual aspiration of adipose tissue is supplemented by vibration, which allows to extract a uniformly required amount of fatty infiltrate. Vibration causes emulsification of adipose tissue, which allows you to carry out the procedure without damaging non-fatty structures.
