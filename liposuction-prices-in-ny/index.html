Liposuction Prices In New York

Liposuction is a surgical correction of local fat deposits, which allows you to get rid of excess fat in the shortest possible time.

Of course, the effect achieved is not permanent and with time, patients who have undergone liposuction procedure can gain weight again if they do not follow diet and play sport.

Nevertheless, this operation allows you to ensure the main thing – a beautiful slim body shape, which is much easier to maintain than to get by means of diets and fitness.

The recovery period after liposuction directly depends on how much fat tissue was eliminated.

With small and medium volumes, recovery is fast enough and with minimal complications.

In particular, almost does not manifest pain, and discharge from the hospital is possible in 1-2 days. With large volumes, pain can be more severe (attacks are stopped with pain medication), and hospital stay can take up to 7 days.

Also, after a significant scale of surgery, the following symptoms may appear:

  • decreased appetite;
  • mild ailment, dizziness;
  • sweating;
  • general weakness;
  • a slight increase in temperature.

After a while, the body’s condition is normalized.

During the week, bandages are regularly performed. Bandages finally removed at 7-10 days.

Bruises and slight puffiness disappear by the end of 3 weeks or earlier. Preservation of possible numbness in the operation area lasts up to 2-3 months.

Mandatory wearing of compression underwear is a month.

Limitations that must be followed after liposuction:

  1. refusal of any loads, sports, physical labor and so on;
  2. ban on visits to saunas, solariums;
  3. ban on sunburn in direct sunlight.

The primary effect of lipoaspiration can be assessed in a month; the final effect is formed after 4-6 months.
