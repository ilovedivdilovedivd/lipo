Statement: Dr. Do is a double board-certified cosmetic surgeon and founder of Aesthetic Surgery Institute in Houston, Tx. With over 20 years of experience, Dr. Do and his team specialize in cosmetic enhancements including face lifts, breast augmentations, breast lifts, mommy makeovers, and more.

Dr. Do graduated from the the prestigious Brown University School of Medicine in 1995, as part of the Program in Liberal Medical Education (PLME), a highly selective, combined MD/BA program that accepts only 50 highschool students worldwide – the only of its kind among Ivy League institutions. After graduating, Dr. Do completed his surgical residence at Boston Medical Center and St. Joseph Medical Center (now part of the Methodist Surgery residency program) and a research fellowship at Boston Medical Center/Boston VAMC.

Dr. Do is board certified by The American Board of Surgery and The American Board of Cosmetic Surgery. He has held previous leadership positions as Trauma Surgeon Director and Chief of Surgery at Oakbend Medical Center and Jackson County Health Center and has been an Oral Examiner for The American Board of Cosmetic Surgery since 2017.

Dr. Do has dedicated his life to the field of aesthetic surgery and has honed his surgical skills by traveling nationally and internationally participating in numerous symposiums, workshops, and fellowships – led by leaders of surgery including Dr. Timothy Martin and Dr. Dean Toriumi.
