Liposuction In Rancho Cucamonga CA

Our clinic is very often used by patients who want to do liposuction in a particular zone.

Such problem areas arise in places where fat deposits locally accumulate and deform the contour and shape of the body or face.

Neither exhausting physical exercises, nor strict diets, unfortunately, in such cases do not help.

After consultation with our specialists, we can offer, depending on the location of the aesthetic defect, such operations: liposuction of the abdomen or the region of the thighs and buttocks, liposuction of the chin or face.

It should be noted that liposuction should not be considered as a quick way to lose weight.

This is really a quick way, but you can get beautiful outlines of the body and model the desired forms that are not given by nature or lost due to an incorrect way of life and nutrition, it is possible only by following the doctor’s recommendations in the future.

In addition, it is important that this method of correction gives stable results, because, in fact, it saves unnecessary cells of adipocytes in places of “fat traps”.

The popularity of liposuction in Rancho Cucamonga, both in women and men, is due to the fact that it does not leave significant defects on the skin, and the rehabilitation process is much faster than with other plastic surgeries.

Indications

Indications for liposuction are local fat deposits that deform the contours and shape of the body part with a relatively normal development of fatty tissue and a slight excess of skin that has not lost its elasticity.

Contraindications

exacerbation of chronic diseases and the presence of diseases in which surgical intervention is not recommended (diabetes, cardiovascular and oncological diseases and liver diseases);

  • mental disorders;
  • pregnancy;
  • excess of inelastic skin and including the skin-fat apron in the zone where the intervention is expected;
  • a greater share of the likelihood that the weight of the patient will change significantly, for example, if there is an intention to lose a large amount of excess weight.

Preparation for surgery

At the consultation, the doctor will listen to your wishes and concerns. It is necessary to tell the surgeon about the diseases that can cause complications: problems with blood coagulability, chronic diseases in the acute stage, hypertension and so on.

The surgeon of our clinic will explain to you which method of liposuction and anesthesia will be used and why this choice is optimal in your case. The stationary conditions will also describe you, tell you what complications may occur, and will acquaint you with all the risks that are possible both during the operation and in the postoperative period.

It should be noted what the doctor will warn you about:

  • weight loss before surgery significantly facilitates the postoperative period and the cosmetic effect in this case is achieved more quickly;
  • after pregnancy, sometimes such an operation as a liposuction of the abdomen is required, but one should not do it in the first year after childbirth, because the abdominal wall will tighten and the skin will shrink, and the hormonal background will change, and it may not be necessary to resort to surgical intervention;
  • it is recommended to stop taking medications containing acetylsalicylic acid or other substances that change blood coagulability, at least a week before the operation;
  • it is recommended not to smoke or smoke no later than 2-3 weeks before the surgery, because smoking disrupts the microcirculation of the blood in the skin and can prolong the healing period of the wounds;

In the morning before the operation you need to refrain from food and water. Once again, there will be an opportunity to discuss all the details with the surgeon, after which he will make the markup of the operating area. Also photos will be taken that will allow you to evaluate the results of correction after recovery.

Operation

Liposuction is performed under intravenous anesthesia, sometimes, under indications, under local anesthesia or endotracheal anesthesia.

The zone of operation is infiltrated with a solution that contains saline, anesthetic, epinephrine and other medications. The composition is injected either with syringes, or with a special device, which feeds the solution into the cannula for infiltration. Cannula is a hollow, thin tube with many holes.

The volume of the injected solution depends on the zone in which liposuction is performed and the tasks of the specific surgical intervention.

I must say that in some cases the volume of the solution that is administered can be up to 0.79-1.05 gallon, but this depends on the surgical method chosen by the surgeon and the amount of fat that will be removed. According to scientific research, a large amount of solution reduces the risk of vascular injury and reduces blood loss, which is very important in this operation to avoid complications.

The size of the incisions corresponds to the size of the cannula and, as a rule, no more than 5 mm. In some cases, there is a need to protect the edge of the cut with a skin attachment to prevent injury during movement.

After the vessels are spasmodic and anesthetized, surgeons start liposuction with one of the methods. After the necessary volume of liposuction is reached, the wounds are sutured and drained. An aseptic dressing is applied, and compression underwear is put on.

Conclusion

With full trust in the doctor, proper preparation and timely treatment of the symptoms that are troubling, complications can be avoided.

The clinic guarantees you the highest professionalism of medical personnel, excellent hospital conditions and care during the recovery period, as well as minimal risks of complications.

And you have the opportunity not only to correct the body shape, but also to get rid of complexes and depression in this regard, to increase self-esteem and confidently move further on the path to an ideal external form.

Postoperative period

It is recommended to rest for up to two days, and then you can return to your usual physical activity, but, of course, you should avoid active sports.

Sutures are removed after a week operation field, but the recovery period lasts longer up to a month, it is recommended to wear compression underwear and undergo a lymphatic drainage massage, which will help to recover faster. It is necessary to avoid visiting the sauna and bath, as well as a solarium and direct sunlight for 6-12 months after the operation.
