Liposuction Cost And Before After Photos In Nevada

Liposuction in Nevada is a surgical operation to remove fat accumulations, which is often resorted to when the fight against extra pounds is unsuccessful. Liposuction is a fairly radical and very effective method by which you can remove excess fat from the abdomen, chest, back, hips, pubic area, hands, and even from some areas of the face, such as cheeks or chin. Liposuction is also good because the connective tissue is formed at the site of the operation, which means that fat in these places will not be postponed any more.

Contraindications to liposuction.

Liposuction helps not for everyone. Juveniles, people with diabetes, as well as patients with complete loss of skin elasticity, do not undergo liposuction.

Also, one should refrain from surgical intervention in the presence of some chronic diseases or during acute manifestations of inflammatory processes.

If liposuction is performed surgically, then it is contraindicated during menstruation, as these days the risk of bleeding increases.

And, of course, contraindications to any kind of liposuction are pregnancy and lactation. Liposuction is not a fight with excess weight and not a way to lose weight, but a radical method of correcting the figure.

That is, liposuction will only help to correct the figure, but it will not relieve you of excess weight.

You can not use liposuction instead of losing weight. First you have to sit on a diet, lose weight to the desired weight, and only then decide what part of the body you need to put in order. Most often it is the thighs and buttocks, and sometimes it can be the face.

The most problematic area for liposuction is the abdomen. Far from always pumping fat will give the belly beauty. If the skin of the abdomen is stretched after childbirth, or the woman has very strongly and sharply lost weight, then the stomach may be permanently crumpled.

Liposuction does not always give a good result. An example of this: Britney Spears, Courtney Love and Tara Reid

What kinds of liposuction are there?

And now we will go directly to the description of the methods of liposuction.

In our country, the most common are vacuum, tumescent and ultrasound techniques. But there is also a new type of surgery, such as liposculpture.

Ultrasonic liposuction in Nevada

Ultrasonic liposuction is not a surgical method of liposuction, but only an external one. Ultrasonic liposuction consists in the destruction of fat cells by the action of ultrasonic waves. At the same time, do not make tissue incisions, and excess fluid can be removed from the body naturally. No scars and bruises, but at the same time the effectiveness is lower, so immediately be ready for repeated sessions.

Tumescent liposuction

Tumescent liposuction is very different from the above methods preoperative preparation. First, a special solution is injected into the fatty deposit zone, which provokes the contraction of the blood vessels and swelling of the fatty deposits.

Before liposuction, the doctor marks the places where the operation will be performed. This preliminary preparation ensures the effectiveness of the effect on the fat cells.

Vacuum liposuction

Vacuum liposuction is surgical liposuction, in which subcutaneous fat is pumped out with the help of a special vacuum suction, used in surgery. The surgeon makes a small cut on the skin, through which a surgical suction, similar to a long needle, is inserted under the skin.

This pump is connected to a container, where the subcutaneous fat is pumped through the tube.

To pump out all the fat, the surgeon makes intensive movements by sucking under the patient’s skin. Vacuum liposuction is the most accessible and widespread of all techniques. In the process of vacuum liposuction, fat deposits are immediately removed by means of a vacuum device, and this is up to 10 liters of fat at a time.

For vacuum liposuction, general anesthesia is used, and for tumescent – local. Also these liposuction are carried out for a couple of hours and do not require a long stay in the hospital. When using surgical techniques, the postoperative period lasts up to three weeks, during which pain can be observed, bruises, swelling.

In the postoperative period, the wearing of special postoperative compression underwear is indicated, so that the updated figure retains the desired shape, and everything inside is healed correctly. Otherwise, the formation of voids at the site of surgery and deformation of the subcutaneous layer is possible.

The effectiveness of removing excess fat with liposuction you can evaluate in six months, although the first results will be visible after 2 weeks.

Laser liposuction in Nevada

Even more modern technology is laser liposuction, during which the fat tissue is destroyed under the influence of a laser. The derivation of the byproducts thus formed also occurs naturally, and only by removing large accumulations of fat can the vacuum suction method be used.

The advantages of laser liposuction are its painlessness and the absence of scars, as well as the fact that laser liposuction causes coagulation – the sealing of blood vessels under the influence of high temperature at the treatment site, which eliminates blood loss and reduces the formation of hematomas.

It is also important that under the action of the laser in the skin collagen and elastin are actively formed – substances responsible for the elasticity of the skin.

Thus, during this procedure, the process of removing fat deposits is combined with skin lifting on the problem area, due to which the aesthetic result is improved and permanently fixed.

Liposuction: price

How much does liposuction cost? It is not easy to answer this question. After all, it all depends on many factors, for example:

  • inpatient stay after surgery
  • cost of anesthesia during liposuction
  • the cost of the liposuction procedure itself.

The price of liposuction itself depends on what zone is treated, and how much subcutaneous fat should be removed. That is, the more fat you need to remove, the more expensive is liposuction.

What is liposculpture?

Liposculpture is a new trend in the field of plastic surgery, and this operation is much more complicated than just liposuction. First, the doctor makes a vacuum, that is, surgical liposuction, and then redistributes pumped fat to other areas of the body where volume is needed. Thus, a body with new shape is obtained.
