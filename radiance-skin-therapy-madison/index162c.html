What is the best filler for volume loss on cheeks and jawline area?
Filler for jawline
Jun 18, 2019
Based on your experience, I would suggest getting a second opinion. Addressing the areas of volume loss in the midface is important to do before filling the nasolabial folds. I would recommend having a consultation with another injector and express what you are hoping to achieve with this treatment. Good luck!
Which CoolSculpting applicator shape and placement would be best for my abdomen?
Coolsculpting
Jun 18, 2019
Thank you for your question. Without seeing actual photos of your abdomen and doing an in person assessment, it is difficult to say which option would be the best for you. Everyone's fat pads are angled and positioned differently. An in person assessment with an experienced provider will help determine which option is the best for your specific goals. Good luck!
How are lip fillers fixed if you are unhappy with results? Is more filler added?
correcting lip filler
Jun 18, 2019
Thank you for you question. There are a couple of different options when it comes to correcting asymmetry from lip injections. One option would be to dissolve some from the other side. If you are happy with the look of the more full side then I would suggest adding some to the deficient side to even them out. Cost would be at the discretion of the injector for dissolving or adding more product. Good luck!
28 months since volbella lip injections, 60% lip volume remains?
longevity

Can I get Juvederm if I have Sleroderma?
scleroderma
Jun 18, 2019
Thank you for your question! For the majority of patients scleroderma is not a contraindication to receiving fillers. I suggest meeting with an experienced injector who will go through full medical history to make sure that you are a good candidate for fillers.
