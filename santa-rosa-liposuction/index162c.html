A basic indication for liposuction in Santa Rosa is the local form of fat deposits. As a rule, in women fat deposits occur in the zone of the hips, abdomen, waist, knees of the chin.

The purpose of liposuction is not to reduce weight, but to correct the figure.

It is worth saying that liposuction can not create a new silhouette, it can only improve the condition of the initial “starting point”.

With a common type of fat deposits, liposuction can be prescribed as an auxiliary method of weight loss, with necessarily conservative treatment.

When you remove a large amount of fat deposits, there is a decrease in body weight. Large amounts of fat removed, usually ends in a loss in weight, but this is not the purpose of the operation.

There are several types of liposuction.

Dry liposuction

This type is carried out without the introduction of a subcutaneous layer of saline solutions. The advantage is the ability to perform an operation with an unchanged fiber contour, which brings to a high accuracy of the operation.

The disadvantage of the method in the pronounced bleeding tissues, in the laboriousness, the impossibility of using a large diameter cannula. When performing dry liposuction, the maximum amount of fat removed is up to 2000 ml.

Wet liposuction

Today it is widely used. The technique is based on the preliminary saturation of subcutaneous fat local site solution of anesthetic with adrenaline local effects. This method facilitates the extraction of adipose tissue, reduces the possibility of injury to tissues during surgery.

Adrenaline helps reduce the diameter of blood vessels, which leads to less tissue loss. This method is widely used in liposuction through ultrasound, laser, radio waves. Wet liposuction allows you to remove up to 5 liters of fat without damage to your health.

Super Wet Liposuction

This method is very different from the first two. The main difference is infiltration with a solution of subcutaneous fat in the area of exposure. This avoids the presence of blood in the aspirate so that large volumes of adipose tissue can be drained without harm to the patient.

Fat in the area of the operation is infiltrated with a solution of adrenaline, anesthetic, carbonate bumper, then evenly introduced over the entire thickness of the tissue. This method is more sparing than the first two, allows you to achieve better results due to the minimum admixture of blood in the aspirate. During the operation, it is possible to remove up to 10 liters of adipose tissue.

Ultrasonic liposuction in Santa Rosa

It is performed by devices that generate ultrasonic pulses that destroy fat. The ultrasonic generator is controlled by a microprocessor, during operation the ultrasonic waves are converted into mechanical vibrations. The power of ultrasound is controlled by the surgeon, which he selects on the control panel of the device.

Technique of the procedure in many respects resembles the previous one. First, infiltration with solution is performed, then the main course of liposuction begins, which is performed by means of cannulas with an ultrasound element at the end. The fat tissue is removed by vacuum aspiration. At the end of the operation, sterile dressings are applied and compression underwear is put on.

Laser liposuction

This method is based on the effect of laser radiation on the fat layer. In the adipose tissue under the skin begins the destruction of the cell membrane and deep tissue warming. This reduces blood loss and stimulates the formation of collagen.

The operation begins with the infiltration of the subcutaneous layer with a known solution. The main course of liposuction is the use of a cannula, at the end of which a light guide that emits a laser beam is fixed. The resulting fat is removed by vacuum aspiration. At the end of surgery and sterile dressings are applied compression garment wear.

Water jet liposuction in Santa Rosa

This method is based on the principle of water jet suction. The device under pressure supplies water under the skin to the fatty tissue, which becomes a dissector for fat, after which the fat emulsion is removed by vacuum suction. Water is supplied under pressure, which makes it possible to effectively affect fat cells, while maintaining the integrity of blood vessels and nerves.

Radio wave liposuction

The device for such liposuction is a high-tech tool, it is based on a high-frequency generator that is controlled by a computer. The operation begins with infiltration of the solution, after which its main stage is performed. After removing the fat, punctures and sterile dressings are applied to the punctures. Compression lingerie is worn on the patient. Thus, the operation of liposuction is performed by modern methods, allowing to remove excess fat deposits without harm to health.
