Is injecting Sculptra superficially and not deeply safe? Will the Sculptra migrate?
Sculptra
Dec 11, 2019
Injecting sculptra too superficially can lead to nodule or granuloma formation. There are however, certain circumstances and scenarios where I would want to inject sculptra a little differently. I would recommend going to someone who has been doing sculptra for awhile.. An advanced injector with increased knowledge of anatomy and understanding of how sculptra builds volume will provide you with optimal results.
Is there a high risk of granuloma formation after Sculptura injections?
Nodule Formation with Sculptra
Dec 11, 2019
Hi there,
Unfortunately, if it is not mixed properly or in advance, the risk of granuloma formation increases with the use of sculptra. The recommendation is that sculptra is massaged 5 x a day for 5 days for 5 minutes. If you follow these guidelines and pick an injector who knows how to properly use and place sculptra, the results are phenomenal!
