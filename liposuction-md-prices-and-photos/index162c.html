Liposuction Maryland Prices And Photos

Today in the arsenal of plastic surgeons there is a powerful weapon that struggles with excess weight and corrects the figure.

This liposuction is the enemy of cellulite and subcutaneous fat. The procedure of liposuction has a local effect on fat.

There are areas on our body where surgery is almost useless. This is the buttocks, shins, pelvis, back, hips, just the places where cellulite appears most often.

But the problem of cellulite is not under the power of liposuction, but to lose weight, it will help in just one operation.

Our body has the reserve fat. This is the most problematic fatty layer. It perfectly tolerates physical activity, fasting, diets.

There are our “reserves” on the hips, in the lower abdomen, buttocks. Remember, these are places, they really lose weight the hardest. Liposuction is simply a rescue for people suffering from a reserve fat.

Types of liposuction in Maryland

Standard liposuction allows you to remove subcutaneous fat with a cannula – a tube with several holes and a vacuum. The tube is injected under the skin through the incisions in the tissues and moves it back and forth.

The incisions are from 5 to 7 mm. Thus, the fat is destroyed, and the vacuum sucks it through the holes in the cannula.

After a standard liposuction, a person can stay in the hospital for up to three days.

Tumescent liposuction is considered the most effective and safe. First, a special cocktail containing saline, adrenaline and ice medicine is injected under the skin. It promotes the thinning of fat, after which the fat is “sucked” with a vacuum. In a small puncture in the skin, the same tube is inserted – a thin cannula through which fat is removed.

It is worth noting that due to tumescent liposuction, during and after the procedure there is no severe bleeding, nerve endings and connective tissue are not damaged. The likelihood of bruises and bruises after the operation, pronounced scars is reduced to zero.

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia. Within a few hours the patient can go home, and a week later will enjoy his appearance and forget about the operation.

Ultrasonic liposuction destroys the fat layer by ultrasonic waves. But before that, a special solution is introduced into the problem zone and only then ultrasound is affected. Fat turns into an emulsion and is removed by vacuum.

Punctures on the skin for this do less than two millimeters. The type of ultrasound liposuction is very rarely used. The fact is that ultrasound often affects not only fat, but also internal organs. Sometimes this leads to a disruption in the functioning of the body and to the death of a person.
Soft lipo-modelling is liposuction, which affects fat with the help of discharges of electric current. The technique of the procedure is similar to ultrasonic liposuction, but instead of a laser, current is used. After the procedure, the patient is about three days in hospital, then another two weeks he is indicate to wear a bandage.

After liposuction

After liposuction for a month, you should wear tight elastic underwear. Since there is an excess of skin tissue. It must be reduced to the required size. If this does not happen, you need to do excision of excess skin.

After liposuction, fever, weakness, seals under the skin, swelling may occur. A month later, all the consequences will disappear.

The amount of subcutaneous fat that was removed by liposuction will not return back within 5 years. In addition, it is noted that after liposuction the weight of a person continues to decrease for some time.

Indications for liposuction in Maryland

Liposuction is indicated to people with local accumulation of fat and excellent elasticity of the skin.

Since after the operation, the skin should contract. The patient’s weight should be slightly higher than the norm.

For one procedure, liposuction can not be removed more than 6lb of fat, this can greatly harm the body. The next liposuction can be carried out only a month after the previous one. Thus, it is allowed to do a maximum of five liposuction.

But all the same more than one liposuction to do or make it is not advised. This procedure does not eliminate the cause of the appearance of excess weight, as well as does not treat obesity, cellulite and stretch marks on the skin.

Contraindications

Do not use the possibility of liposuction. If you suffer from cardiovascular diseases, kidney and lung diseases, as well as if you have a bad blood clotting.

Liposuction can be used even by pregnant women and nursing mothers, but before taking a responsible decision and contacting a plastic surgeon, think, do you really need a small, but surgical intervention in the body?
