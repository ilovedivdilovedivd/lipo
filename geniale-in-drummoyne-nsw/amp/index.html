Liposuction scars healing unevenly - how long can I expect the scars to remain so prominent?
Liposuction Scars Healing
Jun 5, 2022
Scars in the skin will usually reach complete healing within three months. We don't expect any further healing after that time. The raised scar can be treated by Cortisone injection and Ebrium:Yag Laser.

Cryolipolysis post spinal fusion?
Dec 15, 2021
Radiofrequency (RF) depends on strength and distance from rods, but caution is the best option.
