How long after subcision and Radiesse can I resume infrared sauna workouts?
Infared sauna workouts after Radiesse
Nov 15, 2019
There is no reason you cannot resume your infared sauna workouts after Radiesse. In fact, the infared will aid in healing and speed up your recovery time. If you are actively swelling, you may have an initial increase in swelling and then it will resolve. Enjoy your infared sauna, they offer many health benefits.
