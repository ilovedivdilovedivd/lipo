Is it okay to get Botox and the COVID vaccine a month later?
Botox and Covid Vaccine
Jun 15, 2021
It is recommended to wait 4-6 weeks after treatment of a toxin or filler to receive the vaccine to ensure best results. Hope this helps!
Would I be a good candidate for masseter Botox?
Masseter Botox
Jun 15, 2021
Botox is a great use for masseter slimming. It will help to relax the masseter muscle and help contour a square jaw. Good luck!
How many syringes of filler should I get in my cheeks and what kind?
Voluma
Jun 15, 2021
Voluma is a great filler choice for cheeks. It is different for everybody based on how much they will need. It is an option to start with 1 full syringe (1/2 syringe) per cheek, and then re-evaluate in 2 weeks and see if more is needed. Good luck!
Can I use tretinoin while I am lasering my legs? In order to get rid of the strawberry legs?
Retinols and laser
Mar 25, 2021
You need to be off of all retinols for 7 days prior to any laser services. So, depending on how close together your hair removal appointments are, as long as you can discontinue use for the 7 days prior to each, in between services will be ok. Each provider may have their own recommendations, so be sure to check with yours.
Bad Botox experience (first timer) was injected on Jan 30th, how can I avoid bad results in the future
Botox suggestion
Feb 26, 2021
I am sorry to hear you had a bad first experience with Botox! In finding your next injector, getting a referral from friends or colleagues would be a great way to find the right technician for you. Also, most practices have a website and social media that have before and after pictures, almost like a portfolio of their work that you can see before scheduling. Good luck with your next treatment!
